Fashion fans race to ASOS to nab a 30 dupe of the viral 830 studded shoes causing a stir at Lond

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 17, 2024

ANY eagle-eyed fashion fans who were keeping up with the shows at London Fashion Week will have noticed that one pair of shoes was very popular among attendees. 

Alaïa’s Strass embellished ballet flats were donned by multiple modish celebs and influencers, particularly in the stunning black shade. 

Not only do the gorgeous flats look super comfy – the added sparkle means they’re the perfect accessory to give your outfit that extra bit of pizzazz. 

The only thing that’s not perfect about the stunning clogs is the price tag: they come in at a whopping £830, far too expensive for most of us to justify. 

But fear not – eagle-eyed shoppers have spotted a fab dupe on offer on ASOS, which come in a massive £804 cheaper than the top-end pumps. 

The ASOS DESIGN Libra embellished ballets in black are a similar rounded-shape flat and also feature gorgeous studded details.

And the best part is they’re priced at £26, making them a much more affordable alternative to Alaïa’s pair. 

The only real difference is that the designer ones have a few more crystals and feature only one strap across the top, whereas ASOS’s have three plain buckle straps securing them. 

But given they’re £804 cheaper, the discrepancies are hardly worth mentioning!

And since dazzling ballet flats have been declared the “best shoe for autumn” by fashion mags, you might want to pick up a pair quick before they fly off shelves.
