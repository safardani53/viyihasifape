Kevin McKidd And Arielle McKidd Split After Being Together For 5 Years

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, April 21, 2024

Kevin McKidd and Arielle McKidd are divorcing after five years of marriage. Due to irreconcilable disagreements, the latter just filed for divorce. Arielle has sought shared custody of their two children, according to court filings. She indicated that the remaining requirements, such as asset distribution, child support, spousal support, and the restoration of her previous name, must be completed during mediation.

The couple split up five months ago, and Kevin announced the news on social media at the time, noting that they chose after a thorough conversation. The article included photos of Kevin and Arielle with their children and referred to Arielle as “the finest mother to their children.” Kevin McKidd closed the message by urging that everyone wish them the best and that he and Arielle would always be there for one other.

Kevin McKidd’s wife Arielle McKidd is a private chef.

Arielle McKidd, 34, was born on August 19, 1988. She is apparently a chef, however, it is unknown where she now works. She is on social media, but she hasn’t shared much about her personal life. She is a resident of Dix Hills, New York, and graduated from Half Hollow Hills High School East, according to her Facebook profile. Her social media postings are largely about fundraisers to which she has donated in recent years.

Her Facebook postings clearly demonstrate her opposition to racism. Although she is on Instagram, her account is set to private, and her profile has a link to Save Your Soil, which contains images of farm animals and dogs. Arielle’s social media postings also hinted at her love of animals and horseback riding. Details on her parents, work, net worth, and educational history have yet to be published.

Kevin McKidd and Arielle McKidd’s relationship

Kevin and Arielle met in 2016 at a party and dated for two years. In January 2018, the couple married in Malibu’s Calamigos Ranch. Kevin McKidd announced his marriage on his website, saying that select friends and family members were invited. Aiden, their son, was born in 2018, and Nava, their daughter, was born the following year. Kevin previously married Jane Parker, with whom he had two children. The couple split in 2016, and their divorce was formalized the following year.
