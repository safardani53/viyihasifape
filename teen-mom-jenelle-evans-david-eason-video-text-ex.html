Teen Mom Jenelle Evans' fans 'disgusted' as David Eason spotted in family video after he sent secret

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 23, 2024

JENELLE Evans has been slammed after she was seen cuddling on a family trip next to her husband, David Eason.

Fans were upset to see the Teen Mom star buddying up to him, after he appeared to be flirting with his ex-wife in leaked text messages.

That's on top of accusations from his 14-year-old step-son Jace that the 35-year-old attacked him.

Verified texts sent from David's cell phone show him telling his ex-wife, Whitney Johnson, that he dreams about her all the time and wishes he could "turn back the clock" on their relationship.

Almost as quickly as Jenelle, 31, gained sympathy from fans for David's alleged betrayal, she lost it, after sharing a video of the two of them with her other kids Kaiser, 9, and Ensley, 6, on a family trip to an alligator farm.

The MTV star shared a video of the trip to TikTok, kicking it off bundled next to David expressing her excitement.

BREAKING DOWN

Just a few hours earlier, Jenelle broke down in tears on the same platform while discussing betrayal.

Jace was recently found after he ran away from home. Shortly fter that, the teen accused his step-father of attacking him.

David was subsequently charged with child abuse.

That and the flirtatious text messages sent to his ex sent Jenelle into a tailspin.

The couple unfollowed each other on Instagram and Jenelle deleted all of her TikToks with David.

In the new TikTok, Jenelle said she couldn't sleep and woke up at 3.22 in the morning "out of a dead sleep."

After saying she just started crying, she admitted: "I think I'm completely grieving right now and, um, it sucks."

She said that she was feeling betrayed by "every person" in her life, adding that life kept hitting her with "these bowling balls."

Jenelle continued: "It's really hard to deal with all the emotions and I'm just trying to do my best.

"I keep telling myself, 'You're fine, you're fine, you're fine, you're fine,' but then I have these outbursts of just crying, just out of nowhere."

TROUBLING TEXTS

In text messages obtained by the U.S. Sun and verified to have come from David's cell phone to Whitney on her birthday in May of last year- he told his ex: 

"You were perfect. You just deserve so much better than everything you've been through. That's why it makes me so sad. I think about it so much."

"And I dream about you all the time. I don't know how I ended up here instead of where I wanted to be. I don't know how WE ended up here instead of where I wanted US to be."

David then added: "I'm just so upset right now. And I wish I could turn back the clock."

Whitney exclusively told The U.S. Sun that she was "very shocked and surprised," when Jenelle's husband texted her.

Whitney, 35, said outside of this exchange, she and David "had hardly communicated at all because Maryssa had a phone since she was eight years old. 

"So all my communication was through her other than maybe a handful of times."

"It was very surprising not to mention the fact that he was married, still married. I was married, still married.

"It was inappropriate and very unexpected."

PROTECTIVE SERVICES

This development comes after Child Protective Services has once again become involved with Jenelle and David's home life.

Jenelle's eldest child Jace was placed in CPS custody in late September, just months after the MTV star got custody of the teen in March from her mother Barbara, who had taken care of Jace since he was a toddler.

Despite being mired in investigations from CPS and the Columbus County Sheriff's Department after allegations that David assaulted Jace, Jenelle took to her social media accounts to promote new content for her OnlyFans page. 

Last week, a law enforcement source told The U.S. Sun that there is officially a criminal investigation into Jenelle and David after allegations he assaulted the star's son Jace late last month.
