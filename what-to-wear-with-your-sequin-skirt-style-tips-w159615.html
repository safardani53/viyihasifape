What to Wear With Your Sequin Skirt: Style Tips

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

If you think sequins aren’t for you, think again — this season’s glitzy skirt is daytime-appropriate and fit for any holiday soiree. The key to making sparkles look cool (and not cheesy): Pair it with a casual tee, tank, or button-down and neutral heels.

Take Amanda Seyfried’s ensemble at the Givenchy fashion show in NYC. Her easy tank and lack of statement jewels allows the glitz of Givenchy’s fishtail silhouette to take center stage.

A more formal option: The mock neck top and Louis Vuitton skirt that Kate Hudson wore to the Serpentine Gallery in London.

But it’s not all solids and sequins. Tomboys will love the look on Kelly Ripa, who paired a flannel shirt with a pencil skirt by Malene Birger. For an even more casual vibe, swap the heels for flats or even sneakers.

Scroll down for more celeb inspiration on mixing statement skirts with understated tops and shoes!

Kim Kardashian paired her Balmain stunner with Tom Ford stilettos.

An Edie Parker clutch and Saint Laurent pumps complemented Jada Pinkett Smith’s $1,365 Ashish mini.

Cara Santana tucked Forever 21’s graphic tee into a Parker style.

Australian actress Bella Heathcote donned a Thakoon ensemble.
