Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, January 28, 2024

A BUSTY TikToker tried her hand at the latest 'bikini bottoms as a top' challenge - and proved it's not all it's cracked up to be.

The popular trend sees users doubling up their bikini bottoms and wearing them as a swim top.

After getting requests from her TikTok followers to try out the bikini hack, Fashion enthusiast Kristine did just that in a viral video that's been viewed over 13 million times.

Following a tutorial demonstrated by a woman with a smaller cup size, the large-chested woman attempted it for herself.

"I have a very strong feeling that this is going to be a disaster," Kristine prefaced her try-on.

"Keep watching if you want the train wreck to unfold."

Kristine put a black bikini bottom over her head and adjusted the stretchy fabric so it completely covered the right side of her chest.

She repeated the process with a white bikini bottom, this time covering the left side of her chest.

"Absolutely not, no support," she said upon successfully achieving the hack.

"Keep your swimsuit bottoms as bottoms."
