Galina Becker's Bio: Parents, Ethnicity, Age, Height & Sisters

Galina Becker was born on 11 March 1987, in Florida, United States. An American national, she is of Afro-American ethnicity, having come from a mixed race family.

She grew up in San Francisco with her parents Kevin Becker and Mildred Becker, alongside her two elder sisters Molisa and Undine Becker.

Galina Becker, aged 30, stands 5 feet 7 inches tall. She attended the Georgia Tech University and received a degree in management.

Galina’s Career As Athlete & Transition Into Modelling

Galina  Becker was involved in track and field from her early days. She lettered in track for three seasons while at Mt. Pleasant High School in San Jose, California. She also won regional titles during her sophomore and junior years there.

She continued to pursue track and field at Georgia Tech, and during the 2005-2006 season, she performed at the Georgia Tech Invitational, running 14.92 in the 100-meter high hurdles.

After completing her education and the birth of her first child, Galina became a prominent fitness model because of her athletic background. Blessed with perfect height and body measurements to kill, thanks to her athlete days, her transition was a pretty seamless one.
