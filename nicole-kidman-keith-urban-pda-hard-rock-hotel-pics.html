Nicole Kidman &amp; Keith Urban Show Off PDA At Hard Rock Celebration:Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 29, 2024

Nicole Kidman, 52, and Keith Urban, 51 couldn’t be more in love. The two Aussies were spotted at the Grand Celebration event at Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Tampa in Tampa, FL on Oct. 3, where they held onto each other while watching a nerve-wracking high wire performance. Nicole and Keith watched as Nik Wallenda and his mother, Delilah, performed their act between the two buildings of the Hard Rock. At times, the stunt was so hard for the Big Little Lies star to watch that she gripped Keith tightly, while he stood in front of her with his hands up, cheering on the high wire duo!

While they were captivated by the act they were watching, Nicole and Keith were super relaxed in what they wore. It was a major change from their red carpet looks, but the couples’ best and favorite accessory was on full display — the affection and love they have for one another. After 13 years of marriage and two daughters, Sunday Rose, 11, and Faith Margaret, 9, Nicole and Keith are as strong as ever.

Just last January, the couple shared a number of smooches while attending the Australian Tennis Open on Jan. 24. They sat on the sidelines watching the semifinal between American Danielle Collins and Czech player Petra Kvitová. While the action was on the court, Nicole and Keith couldn’t take their eyes — or lips — off of each other. At one point, Keith was spotted leaning over to whisper sweet nothings into Nicole’s ear!

Following a number of high profile relationships, including Nicole’s marriage to actor Tom Cruise and romance with singer Lenny Kravitz, Nicole and Keith began their relationship January 2005. They bumped into each other at the G’Day LA event, where prominent and influential Australians were celebrated for their achievements and contributions. The pair were married on June 25, 2006 in an intimate ceremony. Throughout their marriage, Nicole and Keith have always been incredibly supportive of each other. Keith has stood by Nicole’s side at her film premieres around the world and Nicole has attended every major country music award show with her crooning husband. Above all, fans love to see this couple stay as smitten as teenagers, 13 years after they said ‘I do!’
