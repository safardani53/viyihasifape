Alexis Sanchez's dogs wear Manchester United shirts after arriving at team hotel

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 3, 2024

IF ever you needed proof that a dog really is Man U's best friend then take a paws out of your day to check out these cute snaps from Alexis Sanchez.

The Chilean superstar become the Premier League's highest-paid player by sealing a move to Old Trafford on Monday.

He has wasted no time getting his world famous Golden Retrievers, Atom and Humber, decked out in his new side's kit.

And as you can see, Sanchez is certainly not leading them a-stray as they pup other tail-waggers to shame.

Although you may have expected them to have the number ca-nine on the back, the South American instead opted to go with his own name shirt identity - although we are sure the pair are unlikely to pick a bone with their owner over the matter.

The dog became cult heroes among Arsenal supporters as Sanchez proved he was barking mad for his furry friends throughout his time in North London.

He has shared numerous images in the past of his loveable mutts - although Gooners might be feeling a little woof after the pair's apparent switch in allegiance.

Sanchez is currently living in the Lowry Hotel in Manchester city centre after finally completing his swap deal to United from Arsenal that saw Henrikh Mkhitaryan go in the other direction.

And he has wasted no time in trying to score some points with dog lovers in the red side of town.
