2 Teams, 2 Studios, 2 Furious!

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Abby Lee Miller’s quest to get the Elite Team back on track was a focal point on Dance Moms Season 6 Episode 32 … but was she successful?

If you watch Dance Moms online, you know that the ALDC has been fractured of late as the Elites and the Minis have battled for glory.

Watch Dance Moms Season 6 Episode 32 Online

The Junior Elites have had nothing but problems with Abby on recent episodes of this Lifetime hit and they made it clear they had finally had enough of all the drama going on with her. 

All she ever seems to do is cast them aside, so it makes perfect sense for them to ditch her for good. She doesn’t even act like gives a damn about them at the best of times. 

It’s sad, but there’s clearly favoritism going on in the ALDC and it’s getting a bit much for everyone. The moms decided to reach out to Erin Babbs to train them. If you recall, she is the head if the MDP dance studio. 

Erin and Abby have been at odds all season long due to the competitions, so you can just imagine how happy Erin was to step in and piss Abby off. 

Their first number working with Erin was titled, "And Then There was One." This number would also serve as a performance to deal with the loss of Maddie, Mackenzie and Jojo. The moms decided to take an Instagram picture to show Abby they were not messing around. 

The Mini moms went to check on Abby to make sure she was okay, but she broke down into tears and made a shocking statement. 

"I love those kids as if they were my own. I would lay down in the street and die for some of those kids. I made them into stars. Why don’t the mothers understand that?"

Abby definitely has a funny way of showing it if that is how she feels. All you have to do is look back at the sheer way she speaks to the Elite team. It’s disgusting. 

Abby then dropped the bomb that the Minis and the Junior Elites would be facing off against each other in a competition. Yikes.

Things only seemed to be getting better for the girls who ditched Abby. Nia was offered the opportunity to sing with Alexx Calise. This was a huge opportunity and she politely accepted.

However, Nia completely screwed up the first recording. Her voice was all over the place. It could have been nerves, or even pressure, but she really needed to adapt at that opportunity and she kind of blew it.

Abby then text the moms and begged that one of them would meet her to sort everything out, but the moms really don’t care about her anymore. In their eyes, she was horrible to them.

The Mini moms then went to war with Abby because she dressed Payton in a garbage dress, with actual trash on it. Will these moms ever stop arguing?

At the big night, Abby showed up and started hurling digs at everyone. It was rude and showed just how bitter she was. The moms decided to just ignore her. 

Nia and Kendall managed to prove to Abby she brushed them aside for no reason, by owning their performance. Abby refused to speak.
