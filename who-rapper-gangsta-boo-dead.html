Who was rapper Gangsta Boo?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 12, 2024

LOLA Chantrelle Mitchell made a name for herself as a rapper under the stage name Gangsta Boo.

The Memphis, Tennessee native tragically passed away on January 1, 2023, at the age of 43.

Who was rapper Gangsta Boo?

Gangsta Boo was a popular rapper who first rose to fame as the second female member of the Memphis-based hip-hop group Three 6 Mafia.

After leaving the group in 2001, she launched a successful solo career, which included countless songs and several collaborations with high-profile stars like Juicy J, Outkast, and French Montana, among others.

When Gangsta Boo was not in the studio, she could often be found posting about her life on Instagram to over 259,000 followers.

What was Gangsta Boo's cause of death?

Gangsta Boo was found dead around 4pm on January 1, 2023, according to Fox13.

Her death was later confirmed by Three 6 Mafia founder DJ Paul on Instagram.

"MAN WE WAS JUS TOGETHER 3 WEEKS AGO," he wrote at the time.

"REST WELL QUENN."

Since her death was announced, several fans and industry colleagues have taken to Twitter to remember the late star.

What was Gangsta Boo's net worth?

Over the years, Gangsta Boo was able to amass a fortune thanks to her music career.

At the time of her passing, she had an estimated net worth of $2million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The majority of her worth came from album sales as well as other business ventures.
