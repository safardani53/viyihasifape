Primarks 10 trainers look exactly like ones from Reebok but theyre 55 cheaper

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 5, 2024

PRIMARK is selling trainers that look exactly like ones from Reebok - only they are £55 cheaper.

The budget fashion chain showed off the £10 shoes on their Instagram page and they have already been liked by almost 150,000 followers.

The trainers come in two shimmery shades including a metallic grey and a rose gold and both have chunky white soles.

And it appears they are almost identical to Reebok classic trainers that come in a pearlised pink colour.

But instead of being as cheap as the Primark pair, they are being sold for £65 on the Selfridges website.

And once again fashion fans were eager to get their hands on a pair of the purse-friendly footwear.

One said: "Oh my God. I want!!"

While another said: "Oo I like the pink ones."

Meanwhile others bragged how they had picked up pairs of the shoes and the discount price of a fiver - as they were in a recent sale.

One shopper wrote: "£5 in Primark Middlesbrough #bargain."
