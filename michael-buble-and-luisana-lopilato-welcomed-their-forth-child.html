Michael Buble And Luisana Lopilato Welcomed Their Forth Child

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Michael Buble and his wife Luisana Lopilato are expecting their second child, as the latter announced on social media. On August 19, the 35-year-old Argentine actress came to Instagram to post a photo of her and her husband holding their newborn child’s foot. She captioned it as follows:

“Love gives birth to life, light, and her… our baby Cielo Yoli Rose Bublé. With your 3.8 kilogram, you have finally come into our life!!! Thank you, God, for this immeasurable treasure; we adore you!! Noah, Elias, Vida, and your parents.”

The couple already has three children, Noah (8), Elias (6), and Vida (3), and with Cielo’s arrival, the family of five has grown by one. Michael Buble and Luisana Lopilato’s fourth pregnancy was disclosed in February 2022, after the premiere of the former’s song video, I’ll Not Love You. The couple replicated various romantic memorable sequences from classic films such as Titanic, The Notebook, and Love Actually in the YouTube video.

A peek look at Michael Buble and Luisana Lopilato’s families

Michael Buble and Luisana Lopilato initially met during an afterparty after the singer’s gig in Buenos Aires in 2008. According to a person close to the couple who spoke to People Magazine:

“They met at a reception thrown by his record label following his gig in Buenos Aires. The label’s owner wanted Michael to meet Argentina’s most renowned actress. He stated it was love at first sight when he met her.”

Michael Buble and Lopilato confirmed their engagement in 2010 after dating for over two years. A year later, the couple married in two separate countries, Canada and Argentina. Buble remarked to People Magazine in May 2011 about his second wedding:

“It’s simply another reason to celebrate our love, have a few beers, and dance all night.”

The couple announced in May 2013 that they were expecting their first child together, a boy called Noah, on August 27, 2013. Buble and Lopilato revealed in 2015 that their 22-month-old son will soon have an older sibling, and Elias was born in January 2016. Unfortunately, Noah was diagnosed with liver cancer at the same time, and the family posted the news on Facebook:

“We are heartbroken by our eldest son Noah’s recent cancer diagnosis, for which he is presently receiving treatment in the United States. We have always been loud about the value of family and our affection for our children. Luisana and I have placed our professions on hold to dedicate our whole time and attention to Noah’s recovery.”

The message went on to say:

“We simply ask for your prayers and respect for our privacy at this terrible time. We have a long road ahead of us, and we hope that with the help of family, friends, and admirers all across the globe, we will win this war, God willing.”

Fortunately, Noah came home in March 2017 after 18 months of therapy. A year later, in February 2018, the couple announced their third pregnancy, and in July, Vida Amber Betty was born. Michael Buble was engaged to Debbie Timuss before meeting Luisana Lopilato, but their relationship ended in November 2005. From 2005 until 2008, he was romantically associated with British actress Emily Blunt.
