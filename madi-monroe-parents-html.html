Tiktok Star Madi Monroe Turns 17, Her Mother Posts Birthday Wishes for Her

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Social media star Madi Monroe, who rose to fame with her lip sync videos, songs, and dance clips on TikTok, has turned 17.

The aspiring actress celebrated her birthday and took to Instagram to post her candid birthday pictures. She donned a black tee and a black leather jacket while she looked adorable playing with her birthday cake. The backdrop had purple number balloons that read “17.”

The post was accompanied by a caption that mentioned her date of birth, “012704,” meaning she was born on January 27, 2004.

Monroe also tagged photographer Antonio Schuelke in her post as he snapped those stunning pictures.

Her birthday post received a huge number of birthday wishes from several celebrities and fans. 

“Happy birthday love,” actress and YouTube personality Carrington Durham wrote. Actress Sissy Sheridan, Young UNICEF Ambassador Amelie, make-up artist Amanda Diaz, and TikTok stars Andre Swilley and Darianka also dropped birthday wishes on her post.

Likewise, a fan commented, saying they were of the same age, “No way we almost the same age.” Another fan couldn’t believe Monroe was so young. The fan wrote:

You're only 17?

The social media star has received over 600 thousand likes and a little over 5 thousand comments on her birthday post. She enjoys a massive fan following of 3.7 million on Instagram, as of this writing. 

While Monroe’s fans and celebrities wished the TikTok star by dropping comments on her post, her mother, Erika Monroe-Williams, took to Instagram to post adorable throwback pictures of her daughter.

Madi Monroe and her mother, Erika Monroe-Williams. (Photo: Instagram)

Erika‘s post featured a series of pictures of the mother-daughter duo from the past, along with a heartfelt caption. In the caption, the emotional mother expressed that raising Monroe was a fun experience. Further, she said that she could not be prouder of her daughter as she never stopped learning and experience everything life had to offer.

The full caption read:

@madimonroe‘s birthday celebration continues! What fun it has been to raise you, you have never stopped wanting to experience everything life has to offer- I’m so proud of the person you are, the daughter you are and the friend you are. Happy 17th birthday, Madison!

The TikTok star is the only child of her parents, Erika and Matt Williams (father).

Erika is an American television news anchor and show host who has working experience as a main anchor and health reporter for Fox affiliate KJTV-TV. She worked as the anchor of TV shows Good Evening Arizona and The News Show at 3TV.

Currently, she works as the founder, manager, and producer at artists management and production company Moxie Artists.

Monroe’s father, Matt, is a former third baseman and a professional baseball manager. He played for the San Francisco Giants, Cleveland Indians, and Arizona Diamondbacks in Major League Baseball.
