Maura Higgins struts her stuff in lingerie and suspenders in sexy Ann Summers ad

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

MAURA Higgins sent temperatures soaring as she strutted her stuff in sheer lingerie and suspenders to promote Ann Summers.

The Love Island favourite oozed confidence as she modelled a range of the sexy underwear brand's goods in the sultry ad.

Maura, 30, is seen walking a mini catwalk as she paces up and down to the 80s hit Need You Tonight by INXS.

At one point, she teases viewers with a glimpse of her scantily clad behind, while working up a variety of poses for the camera.

Her outfits range from a red lace two-piece, to an all-black ensemble completed with matching stockings and stiletto heels.

At times the stunning brunette lays down on a padded stool and plays around with a feather boa.

Showing her playful side, the one-time Dancing on Ice contestant moves her breasts up and down in time to the beat of the music.

She captioned the video: "Sunday strut @annsummers".

Maura is currently enjoying a romance with fellow Love Islander Chris Taylor and recently revealed she hopes he will pop the question.

Irish Maura told The Sun on Sunday: “We’re very much in love and we use the L-word a lot. I look into the future and see myself with a family.

“I don’t want a baby any time soon, but when I’m ready and settled.

"I want to be married before I have a baby, so Chris better hurry up and give me that engagement ring, because I’m not getting any younger.”

The couple became good friends after appearing on the 2019 series of Love Island. As the pair spent more time together last summer, fans would comment on social media that they would make the perfect couple.

They went public in November, posting a loved-up snap on Instagram from their trip to Dubai to celebrate Maura’s birthday.

At the time Maura’s ex-boyfriend, ballroom dancer Curtis Pritchard, 24, accused the pair of stabbing him in the back.

Maura and Curtis had coupled up in reality show’s Majorcan villa, eventually finishing fourth on the ITV2 dating show. But they split seven months later.

She insists her romance with Chris blossomed out of friendship after her break-up from Curtis.
