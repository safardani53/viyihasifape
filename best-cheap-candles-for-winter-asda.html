Asda shoppers go wild as huge candles are marked down to 35p - their lowest price EVER

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

A GORGEOUS scented candle is an absolute must-have for the colder months ahead. 

As you snuggle up against the cold on the sofa, what could be nicer than your living room being filled with a delicious aroma? 

And eagle-eyed shoppers are heading to Asda to pick up some scented candles perfect for winter that are scanning for next-to-nothing prices.

Canny consumer Caroline shared the fab find in the Facebook Group Extreme Couponing and Bargains, which has a whopping 2.3m members. 

The savvy shopper posted a picture of her candle haul from Asda, to make others aware of the mind-boggling deal currently on offer.

Caroline had first picked up five of the Pacific Wax Co Vanilla Shortbread Jar Candles. 

Read more shopping stories

The scent is described on Asda’s website as: “Tempting buttercream frosting sparkling with sugar granules.” 

It also has notes of butter, whipped cream and fresh-baked cupcakes, making it good enough to eat. 

Each 510g piece burns for 85 hours, meaning you’ll get plenty of use out of the bargain buy.

And while each would normally set you back £3.50, Caroline picked them up for just 35p each.

That’s a tenth of the usual price, meaning the savvy shopper nabbed all five for less than just one would normally cost. 

But the huge savings didn’t end there: Caroline had also nabbed two of the Eco Wix Ombre Grey Spa Waters Large Jar Candles for a fraction of their usual price. 

Each burns for around 60 hours, and features a “refreshing blend combining sweet orange and ripe peach with a splash of marine notes”, the supermarket’s website describes. 

While each would normally be priced at £8, Caroline paid just 80p for each – once again, just a tenth of the normal cost. 

Her post racked up hundreds of likes and comments, with users dashing to Asda to get their hands on the cut-price candles. 

“I’m so jealous,” one wrote alongside laughing emojis. 

“Get to Asda for me,” another joked while tagging their friend. 

If you’re looking to stock up on candles for winter, why not check out TKMaxx, where there’s a fab dupe of a designer favourite.
