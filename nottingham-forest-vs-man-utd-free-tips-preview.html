Nottingham Forest vs Man Utd preview

It was a Boxing Day to remember for Nottingham Forest and Manchester United.

The former were impressive against a toiling Newcastle side at St James' Park, coming from behind to win 3-1.

Chris Wood notched an impressive hat trick and they could have scored more in truth.

While United also did it the hard way by coming from behind, this time against high-flying Aston Villa.

Erik ten Hag went odds-on to be the next managerial casualty during half time when 2-0 down at Old Trafford.

But an Alejandro Garnacho inspired United came out swinging in the second half, with Rasmus Hojlund grabbing the winner in the 3-2 win.

United have won the last 11 meetings between the sides in all competitions - stretching back to 1996.
