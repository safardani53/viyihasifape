Jada Pinkett Smith Celebrates New Years Eve In Blue Floral Bikini Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 26, 2024

Jada Pinkett Smith bid adieu to 2020 with one last bikini pic on Instagram! In the snap, which the 49-year-old actress posted on December 31, New Year’s Eve, Jada knelt on the sand and gave the camera a gorgeous glance, while the sun beamed behind her on a beautiful, cloudless day. The Girls Trip star’s short blonde pixie cut looked super cute, but Jada’s two-piece was truly the standout!

The actress sported a blue, floral string bikini with a few hints of white detailing in the pattern. Ever the fashionista, Jada accessorized her beach day look with layered gold necklaces. It even appeared as though Jada was hardly sporting one drop of makeup, opting for a fresh-faced appearance! Along with the gorgeous snap, Jada also penned a caption perfect for the end of 2020.

“I welcome you 2021 with open arms,” she began the caption to her post. “I have no expectations. I’m going to be still and grateful,” the star mused, adding two sparkling emojis along with a dazzling pink heart emoji. Jada’s fans and millions of followers absolutely adored the photo and her caption, and left a string of compliments throughout the comment section of her post! 

2020 was quite an interesting year for Jada. There were definitely a lot of ups and downs. Red Table Talk was really the focus of Jada’s professional work throughout the year. She used the platform as a way to have meaningful conversations about race in America, the ongoing coronavirus pandemic, and even the college admissions scandal.

But there were also some trying moments. July 2020 marked a pivotal moment in the star’s life, when Jada and her husband, Will Smith, acknowledged some of the difficulties in their marriage. The beloved couple talked things through on Red Table Talk and came out stronger than ever. Not only does today, December 31, mark New Year’s Eve, it’s also Jada and Will’s 23rd wedding anniversary! There’s no doubt that Jada is ready to celebrate, say goodbye to 2020 and move forward with a fresh start into a brand new year!
