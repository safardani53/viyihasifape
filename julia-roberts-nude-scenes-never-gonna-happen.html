Julia Roberts Nude Scenes: Never Gonna Happen

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 1, 2024

Last week, we learned the disappointing news that we will no longer see Brad Pitt nude on screen. The actor takes his role model status seriously.

On a similar note, fans better enjoy Julia Roberts in a bathing suit in her upcoming movie, Charlie Wilson’s War, because that’s as close to nude as she’s gonna get!

There’s a reason why we don’t see Roberts naked or even running around in bathing suits in movies, the 40-year-old actress tells E! “It’s just not my thing.”


Julia Roberts nude scenes: Not her thing and not gonna happen.

We know what you are thinking. You saw Julia Roberts nude in Pretty Woman. Sorry, you’re wrong. She used a body double to shoot nude scenes even then!

The mother of three, who welcomed her youngest son, Henry, in June, has always closely guarded her private life with her husband, Danny Moder.

There’s certainly nothing wrong with her desire to keep her clothes on at work, either – despite how enjoyable seeing Julia Roberts naked might be.
