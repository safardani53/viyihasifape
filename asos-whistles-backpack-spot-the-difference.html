Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 24, 2024

WHEN it comes to leather handbags, you can't go wrong with a chic black backpack.

And while we've been pining after this Whistles rucksack for what feels like forever, you can now get an amazing ASOS offering for a staggering £155 less...

Part of Whistles' core collection, the Verity backpack is the ultimate in effortlessly cool dressing.

With its fold-over zip detail and miniature style, this adorable bag looks great paired with boyfriend denim on lazy weekends.

Alternatively, dress it up with smart trousers and heels come Monday morning.

But priced at a hefty £180 for a bag that just about fits a book and not much else, this impractical purchase has remained firmly on our fashion lust lists.

  • Black Backpack with Rose Gold Zip, £25 from ASOS  - buy now

Now luckily for us, ASOS has launched an amazing dupe of the timeless style for only £25... and we have to say, we like it even more than it's luxury inspiration.

Featuring the same zip foldover in a beautiful shade of rose gold, our hearts skipped a style beat when we spotted this stunning ASOS offering.

Heavily inspired by the Whistles design, the ASOS backpack boasts a faux-suede finish as well as a scuba style lining to keep things feeling modern and cool.

  • Whistles Mini Verity Leather Backpack, £180 from John Lewis - buy now

What's more, not only is the high street offering a total steal in comparison to the Whistles leather version, you're also getting much more for your money.

Just think of how many magazines, books, and lip balms you can lug around in this beautiful bag. Winning.
