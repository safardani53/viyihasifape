Man in his 20s stabbed to death in Hackney in yet another knife murder in Lawless London

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, March 11, 2024

COPS have launched a murder investigation after a man was stabbed to death in yet another Lawless London knife murder.

The man - thought to be in his twenties - was knifed on Kingsland Road in Hackney, North London, at 6:30am this morning.

An Air Ambulance rushed him to a hospital in Central London, where he died of his injuries shortly after arrival.

A Scotland Yard spokesman said: "Police were called to Kingsland Road, junction with Waterson Street, E2 at 06:29hrs following reports of a man injured.

"He was taken to a central London hospital in a critical condition. He died in hospital a few hours later.

"His next of kin have been informed. A post-mortem examination will be held in due course. Formal identification awaits.

"A crime scene remains in place. As part of this scene, temporary road closures are also in effect and motorists are advised to avoid the area.

"No arrests have been made. Enquiries into the full circumstances are ongoing."

The stabbing comes in a year of brutal knife attacks as bloodthirsty gangs vie for control of the capital's streets.

Last week Lawless London reached a new horrifying milestone after five killings saw the murder toll top last year's.
