Bad Bunny ticket updates The Most Wanted Tour exclusive presale underway today - see seats and p

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 4, 2024

Check for a code before presale begins

There are no guarantees that registration will result in a code, according to Ticketmaster.

A random lottery selects fans who registered to participate, meaning it didn’t matter when you submitted a registration as long as it was before the October 22 deadline.

Just like there are no guarantees that those who registered will get a code, there are no promises that those who got a code will secure tickets.

There is limited availability and more registrations than tickets on sale.

You will know if/when you get a code with an email that should've been received on October 24, telling you whether you were selected to gain access or were put on a waitlist.

If you're selected, you should've gotten a text with a unique code; if put on a waitlist, you might have a chance to snag tickets later.

Why fans had to register for presale

Ticketmaster has released a list of reasons why artists prefer having a registration period for presales.

They are to:

  • Help block bots and scalpers
  • Get more tickets to real people who want to attend the show
  • Reduce resale (only 5-10% of tickets using registration are resold vs 20-30% of others)
