THERE'S no shortage on what can be cooked in an air fryer.

And one air fryer whizz has revealed how she gets perfect crispy pork chops everytime.

Air Fry Jen regularly shares her air fryer recipes on TikTok and has accumulated over 100k followers and 700k likes thanks to her easy-to-follow recipes.

In one such video, Jen, who is a mum-of-two, revealed exactly how she gets her pork chops cooked in less than 15 minutes.

She first put three pork chops flavoured with salt and pepper in the lower tray of her air fryer.

The mum then added three more onto the top tray before seasoning them.
