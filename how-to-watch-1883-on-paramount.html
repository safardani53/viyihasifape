How To Watch 1883 On Paramount+

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

The story of the Duttons begins here. Taylor Sheridan's 1883 is now streaming, only on Paramount+.

Attention, Yellowstone fans! There is a new prequel series that tells the origin of the Dutton family — and it’s now streaming exclusively on Paramount+.

1883, a Yellowstone origin story, follows the Dutton family as they embark on a journey west through the Great Plains toward the last bastion of untamed America. From Academy Award nominee Taylor Sheridan, co-creator of Yellowstone, 1883 is a stark retelling of Western expansion, and an intense study of one family fleeing poverty to seek a better future in America’s promised land — Montana.

Who stars in 1883?

1883 features an acclaimed cast. Western veteran actor and Academy Award nominee Sam Elliott plays Shea Brennan, the cowboy guiding the family across the treacherous American landscape.
 
Country music superstars Tim McGraw and Faith Hill bring the patriarch and matriarch of the Dutton family to life. Isabel May stars as Elsa Dutton, the tough-as-nails Dutton daughter. 

How historically accurate is 1883?

The scope and scale of this series is unlike any historical drama on television. From building an entire 1800s town to authentically crafting every wardrobe, gun and saddle — Sheridan and Paramount+ have ensured that this will be a story for the ages. 

Seeing Yellowstone is not necessary to enjoy this standalone Western epic, but fans of the Paramount Network series will glean extra meaning from the riveting struggle of the Dutton ancestors.
