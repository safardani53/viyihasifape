Steve Toussaint Will Be Featured In House of the Dragon

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

House of the Dragon, an HBO Game of Thrones prequel, starring Steve Toussaint as Lord Corlys Velaryon. Corlys Velaryon, the ruler of House Velaryon, is a renowned sailor. Toussaint, who plays Velaryon, is a well-known British actor who has appeared in films such as Shooting Dogs and Judge Dredd.

House of the Dragon, the highly anticipated Game of Thrones prequel, is set centuries before the events of the renowned fantasy series and examines the deadly Targaryen civil war. The first episode debuted on August 21, 2022. Continue reading to learn more about actor Steve Toussaint and his role in House of the Dragon.

Steve Toussaint has appeared in a number of films.

Steve Toussaint was born on March 22, 1965, in the United Kingdom to Barbadian parents. He apparently took his surname after Toussaint Louverture, the legendary leader of the Haitian Revolution. Toussaint’s film and television career took off in the 1990s, with parts in Judge Dredd, ID, and The Memoirs of Sherlock Holmes, to mention a few. Toussaint has been in a number of notable television episodes and films throughout the 2000s, including Shooting Dogs, Flight of Fury, Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time, Line of Duty, and many more.

In House of the Dragon, Steve Toussaint now plays the key role of Lord Corlys Velaryon. Corlys Velaryon, the leader of House Velaryon, is a well-known sailor. He is the husband of Princess Rhaenys Targaryen. Velaryon is recognized for being astute and loyal, and he is not afraid to speak his honest and forthright ideas. Velaryon is described as “the head of a strong family with ancient origins” in HBO’s official character guide. According to the description,

“The wealthiest man in Westeros, his great fortune includes the world’s biggest fleet, putting him in a prominent position at court. He is the Master of Driftmark, an island in Blackwater Bay west of Dragonstone that serves as the seat of House Velaryon.”

House of the Dragon narrative and episode 1 information

House of the Dragon, one of the most anticipated series of the year, transports fans to the events before the Game of Thrones tale. The story elements will not conflict with Game of Thrones, and viewers can anticipate the program to delve into the backstories of various characters from George R. R. Martin’s weirdly gorgeous fantasy universe. According to HBO’s quick summary of the show:

“The rule of House Targaryen begins: House of the Dragon, Game of Thrones prequel, is here. Based on George R.R. Martin’s “Fire & Blood,” the series covers the narrative of House Targaryen 200 years before the events of Game of Thrones.

The first episode of the program, titled The Heirs of the Dragon and debuting on August 21, 2022, takes its time establishing the major narrative, location, and numerous characters. The episode was well praised by both spectators and critics, who praised the show’s sophisticated narrative, graphics, and performances. Remember to watch House of the Dragon on HBO Max.
