Ciara Adorns Her Bare Baby Bump with Gold Leaf

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

Pregnancy is golden for Ciara!

The soon-to-be mother of four, 38, attended the world premiere of The Color Purple in Los Angeles on Wednesday, showing off her bare baby bump in an eye-catching ensemble.

Ciara — who plays the role of Nettie Harris in the new film adaptation of the Tony-winning Color Purple musical — wore an all-white outfit that consisted of a long cape, flared pants and a flowing blouse unbuttoned to give a peek of her bump.

Elevating the look even further, Ciara adorned her bump with a variety of gold leaf pieces.

Posing on the purple carpet at the Academy Museum, the “How We Roll" singer displayed a bright smile as actress Taraji P. Henson planted a kiss on her bump while costar Colman Domingo stood behind them.

Ciara also held onto director Blitz Bazawule’s hand and gathered for a group photo with Henson, 53, singer H.E.R and actress Danielle Brooks.

Sharing clips on her Instagram story, the star reposted a video of her and Blitz making a strong entrance as they walked hand in hand. She was also captured joining her castmates onstage for a performance bow. 

Ciara also reposted a video of her FaceTiming her daughter Sienna to surprise her with a hello from Alicia Keys. “Alicia calling Sienna with me. Sisi is obsessed. You’re the sweetest. Love you @aliciakeys.” 

Ciara is expecting her fourth child and third baby with her Denver Broncos quarterback husband, Russell Wilson. The couple, who tied the knot in 2016 announced in August they were expecting another baby by sharing a black-and-white video of Ciara dancing with her baby bump.

While the pair share son Win Harrison, 3, and daughter Sienna Princess, 6, Ciara is also the mother of 9-year-old son Future Zahir, who she shares with her ex, rapper Future.

The “1,2 Step” singer, who often posts fun videos on social media, shared a TikTok video last month of her and her eldest child dancing to 310Babii's "Soak City."

Taking turns to dance, Ciara wrote in the caption, "Mommy and Son fun.”
