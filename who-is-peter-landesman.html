CREATIVE Peter Landesman has worn many hats over his longstanding career - some of which include journalist, novelist, and painter.

However, fans of the political activist want to know more about his personal life.

Who is Peter Landesman?

Peter Landesman was born in New York City on January 3, 1965.

The 58-year-old is known for being a director, writer, and artist.

He has written cover stories for magazines including The New York Times, The New Yorker, and The Atlantic Monthly regarding topics like sex trafficking, the refugee crisis, and more.

Landesman has also reported on conflicts in countries like Kosovo, Rwanda, Pakistan, and Afghanistan.

In 1995, published his first fiction book, The Raven, which won him an American Academy of Arts and Letters Sue Kaufman Prize for First Fiction.
