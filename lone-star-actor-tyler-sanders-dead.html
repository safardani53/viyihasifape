Who was 9-1-1: Lone Star actor Tyler Sanders?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 10, 2024

TYLER Sanders made a name for himself in the entertainment industry after starring in The Rookie, Just Add Magic, and 9-1-1: Lone Star.

On Friday, June 17, 2022, it was announced that he passed away at the age of 18.

Who was 9-1-1: Lone Star actor Tyler Sanders?

Tyler Sanders was known as an Emmy-nominated actor who gained prominence after starring in Amazon's successful spin-off, Just Add Magic: Mystery City.

He began his career at the age of ten, after starring in JLW Academy and the short Little Socrates.

When Tyler was not on set, he could often be found posting about his life and fitness journey on Instagram, where he had amassed over 12,000 followers.

Just days prior to his death, he posted pictures in Vail, Colorado, with the caption: "Styling."

According to his Instagram feed, he was also friends with The Secret Life of the American Teenager alum, Jordan Fisher.

On May 10, 2022, Tyler posted a picture of the two of them with the caption: "Life is good. Also Jordan Fisher is a lovely person."

Jordan also commented on Tyler's most recent photo with a heart-eye emoji.

What was Tyler Sanders' cause of death?

Tyler's death was first reported by TMZ.

According to the media outlet, he died in Los Angeles at home, but the cause of death is currently unclear.

More information is expected to come following an autopsy.

Tyler's rep told TMZ that his death is being investigated and he is being remembered as a "good kid" who came from a "good family."

What was Tyler Sanders' net worth?

Tyler's career was just starting to take off before his death, but he was still able to amass a small fortune due to his previous roles.

While his exact net worth is unclear, it is said to be between $250,000 and $300,000.

Prior to his passing, he was working on the upcoming short, Shock!, and the upcoming film, The Price We Pay, which are both scheduled to be released in 2022.
