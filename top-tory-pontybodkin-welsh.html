Top Tory calls for urgent investigation into 'Puma of Pontybodkin' prowling Welsh countryside 'befor

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, February 3, 2024

A TOP Tory has called for an urgent investigation into sightings of a "puma" prowling the Welsh countryside "before someone gets hurt."

Darren Millar said the multiple reports of a big cat dubbed the "Puma of Pontybodkin" are "a real concern".

In the latest sighting, a mystery big cat believed to be larger than a sheep was spotted close to where the beast is feared to be stalking.

The large black creature, spotted in Trelogan, Flintshire, was described as "the size of a sheep and possibly even bigger" as it roamed through a field.

And multiple sightings have been reported of a large puma-like creature skulking ground near the I'm A Celebrity castle in the Welsh hills.

Mr Millar, Clwyd West's Senedd representative, urged the Welsh Parliament to launch an immediate probe into the "worrying" sightings.

'REAL WORRY'

He said: "You'll be aware that in north-east Wales there have been a spate of sightings of big cats in the Welsh countryside in recent months.

“Many members of the farming community in particular are concerned about the impact that these may have on their livestock in the future."

He added: "It is a real worry that seven years on we are still getting reports of big cats being spotted in our countryside here in North Wales.

"A thorough investigation is therefore needed urgently before someone is hurt or worse.”

The most recent incident was filmed by shocked onlooker Jonathan Terry - who was amazed after seeing the animal close to his home.

A monitoring group called Puma Watch North Wales was set up after a series of mystery sheep killings across the countryside.

Jonathan reported the sighting to the group after being able to film it from two fields away.

He said: "I saw it out of the corner of my eye through the kitchen window overlooking the fields behind the house.

"Filmed as I have been reading about these sightings and it looked far too big to be a regular cat and was more like the size of a sheep or possibly bigger."

During the footage, Jonathan says: "That's massive".

A woman can be heard saying: "It is massive because obviously you can see it from here. It’s stalking something."

It is the latest sighting of an apparent big cat in the north Wales countryside.

In January, witnesses described seeing “a large cat roughly the size of a Labrador” walking along a busy road near Talacre, Flintshire.

It followed other sightings of a black “dog-sized” cat 20 miles away in Pontybodkin.

One was reported by terrified Toby Matthews who said he came face-to-face with a big cat while walking through woodland at night.

He recalled: “Maybe 50 or 60 feet away was an enormous black cat looking right at me.

"I took a photo then turned around and quickly walked back. I wanted to start running but was worried it’d give chase.

“I’m convinced that it was a puma. It was about two or three times the size of my little Labrador."

Tony Jones, of Puma Watch North Wales, said the most recent sighting was within two miles of other recent reports of apparent big cats.
