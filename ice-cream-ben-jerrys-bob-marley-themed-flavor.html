Ice cream fanatics beg for Ben and Jerry's to bring back missing Bob Marley-themed flavor that 'stol

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

ICE cream fans are looking for the Ben & Jerry's flavor based on Bob Marley to come back to the shelves.

One person on Twitter asked ice cream lovers to name their favorite flavor from the Vermont-based ice cream company, Ben & Jerry's.

Although there were popular flavors named such as Strawberry Cheesecake and Americone Dream, one user named the flavor called One Love.

This limited edition pint was first introduced in the United Kingdom in 2016 and made its first United States appearance in 2017, according to Food & Wine.

The ice cream flavor is inspired by the late singer, Bob Marley.

The goal was to pay homage to both him and his message of world peace and his vision to make the world a better place.

Read more on ice cream

With each pint sold, Ben & Jerry's decided to donate a portion of the profits to Jamaica’s One Love Youth Camp.

The fan-favorite had a banana ice cream base with caramel and graham cracker swirls, with fudge chunks shaped like peace signs.

The company did temporarily bring the flavor back to its website in late 2018, but it is currently not available for purchase.

Fans have expressed their disappointment at the loss of this unique flavor.

"Ben and Jerry's discontinued one love ice cream. I miss it every day," one tweet read.

"I wish they’d bring back Ben and Jerry’s One Love," another tweet echoed.

Some people got specific explaining why they loved (and still love) the One Love flavor.

"Nothing slaps for me harder than banana-flavored food and there frankly just isn't enough in existence, when the Ben and Jerry's one love ice cream got deleted, I was devastated," someone tweeted.

"That is a limited edition and sometimes hard to find but it’s Bob Marley’s one love by Ben and Jerry’s and if you like banana pudding with nilla wafers then you’ll LOVE this!" another person tweeted about the ice cream.

One person even went as far as tweeting that if they could only keep one ice cream flavor, it would be the Bob Marley pint from Ben & Jerry's.
