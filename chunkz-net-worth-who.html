Who is Chunkz and what is his net worth?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 6, 2024

POPULAR YouTuber and influencer Chunkz has seen great success since he first came onto the YouTube scene.

In line with his ever-growing celebrity status, his bank balance has grown too!

Who is Chunkz?

Chunkz is a social media personality and presenter.

Born on February 21, 1996, the influencer shot to fame thanks to his hilarious content on YouTube.

The comedian was born in northwest London, England and is of Somali descent.

He began filming videos in 2015 with his cousin and fellow YouTuber, Darkest Man, sharing clips of vlogs, pranks, and partaking in challenges with his pals.

His culinary series, Cooking with Chunkz, also caused a huge buzz - so much so, that he dropped out of university in 2016 to focus on his channel.

His gamble paid off, as he continued to create success for himself when he formed the formidable duo that is Chunkz and Yung Filly.

The pair have massively expanded their audience and thanks to their persistence, they have earned gigs with both FootAsylum and Sky Sports.

Alongside Michael Dapaah, in March 2018 Chunkz helped launch a voice app for Google Assistant and Trainline.

Chunkz also won the International Somali Award for Best Entertainer in the same year.

In 2019, Chunkz joined the YouTube group, Beta Squad, alongside Niko Omilana.

He then went on to sign a record contract and released two tracks in 2020.

He bagged a MOBO award in 2019 before later co-presenting the awards ceremony with Maya Jama.

In September 2022 Chunkz got his first show on ITV2 alled Bad Chefs.

In 2023 he played for the England side for the charity Soccer Aid Football match.

The annual charity football match took place at Old Trafford on June 11, 2023.

What is his real name?

Although most know him as Chunkz, his real name is Amin Mohammed.

His nickname is as widely recognised as his hilarious online content.

It was even reinvented by fellow presenter Maya Jama who once dubbed him "Chunkell".
