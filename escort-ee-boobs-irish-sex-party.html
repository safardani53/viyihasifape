Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 8, 2024

AN ESCORT caught in a shocking x-rated video of an Irish sports club "sex party" celebration has revealed what really happened after going viral.

Fifi, who is from Naples in Italy, says she slept with one man who attended the wild Kilkenny hurlers' Gaelic Athletic Association (GAA) club victory party on Tuesday night.

The 32-year-old was seen ripping off her bra and wrapping it around a reveller's head while other pictures showed her dipping her 40EE breasts in the championship trophy.

She's since revealed how her business has boosted 70% since the saucy footage was published this week and claims to have slept with two more men from the party since.

The former accountant, who has been working as an escort for two years since moving to Ireland, says that she and a colleague were paid £355 to attend the party between 8pm and 9.30pm.

Fifi, who did not want to reveal her real name, said she was told the private event - attended by more than 20 men - was a joint cup win and a 21st birthday.

St. Patrick's Ballyragget triumphed in the Intermediate County Final in Kilkenny last Sunday after defeating Graigue-Ballycallan by 1-19 to 2-10.

Soon after the party, images began to emerge of them celebrating their victory and downing pints alongside the scantily-dressed Fifi and her pal.

Speaking to the Irish Mirror, she said: "I come here to make Irish men happy. I always make happy. I like my job.”

Asked if she had sex with any of the revellers at the party, Fifi said: “I only had sex with one guy that night.

“It didn’t happen in the pub. They have some hotel as well upstairs.”

She said that the following night, two men who had attended the party made contact with her and they met up for sex.

She claims neither were fussed about the negative publicity surrounding the event and paid €200 (£177) for one hour each.

Fifi says customers have since been calling her up to find out if she is "the girl from the pictures and from the videos".

But she's not shy of the attention, and says she's hoping the publicity will lead to more cash.

Speaking to the Irish Sun earlier this week, Fifi said she didn't realise her antics were being recorded as she described the attendees as "crazy".

“Oh my God it was mad! I drank too much, way too much alcohol," she said.

“It was all so funny and everyone had a good time. They are funny guys. I didn’t realise they were filming the whole time.

“They know how to party. I think it finished up at maybe 5am.

“I had never been to a GAA party before. It was so funny.”

She said: “I have moved around a lot. I’ve lived in Belfast, Cork and Athlone. But I like Kilkenny. The weekends are fun and very busy, there are a lot of stag trips.”

Hundreds of people took to social media after the footage began circulating online and cracked jokes about the saucy bash.

One joked: “Some pups in Ballyragget. #intermediateCelebrationsGoneWild”.

Another quipped: “Finally. My time has come to create the perfect hashtag. I’d say there are a few lads waking up with #ballyregrets this morning #ballyragget”.

And others added: “Just stuck my transfer papers in there to join #Ballyragget. They seem to have a good set up”.

In response to the footage, club secretary John Glennon told the Irish Sun: “The club will co-operate fully into any investigation into the matter.”

A spokeswoman for the GAA said they were aware of the footage but would not be commenting.

Kilkenny hurling legend DJ Carey said: “I am ­surprised and disappointed, ­something like this shouldn’t happen.

"But I can’t see it being the club’s fault. I would say the club is quite embarrassed about it.”

And he added: “If that tickles your fancy, that’s up to you...I’m not going to be the moral standing ground for anyone. I’m sure it’s a bit of craic. Everyone to their own, enjoy whatever you enjoy.

“I haven’t seen (the vid) so I can’t comment much. If lads are celebrating it’s fine but a cup definitely shouldn’t be involved.”
