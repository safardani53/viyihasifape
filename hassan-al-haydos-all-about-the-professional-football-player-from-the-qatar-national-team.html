Hassan Al Haydos- All About The Professional Football Player From The Qatar National Team

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 30, 2024

Hassan Al-Haydos is a professional footballer. Since 2007, he has been a member of the Qatar Stars League team Al Sadd. It is his first professional club service in 15 years. He has also represented Qatar on the national team since 2008. He is the Qatar national football team’s captain. As a striker, he plays for both the club and the national team.

His Football Career Journey

Hassan was not a football player from the start. Before football, he was interested in tennis. He used to play tennis in elementary school and in the neighborhood. When Al Sadd’s coach, Abdullah Al Shamlan, spotted him playing tennis, he suggested that he try football instead of tennis. Hassan agreed with Abdullah and went to football practice with him.

He is nearing the conclusion of his football career.

In an interview, Hassan said that it was not an easy trip to continue playing football throughout his early professional career. At the time, the club authorized just six professional players to play, and the coaching staff predicted that Hassan would never become a football player. The club’s personnel also urged he go on loan or depart. These punishments depressed Hassan, but the captains of the two clubs, Mohammed Al Ammari and Issa Al Ghanim, stood by him and insisted that he continue in the club. They stated he had the ability to play for the club and that he will be a major player in the future years.

Will Hassan Al-Haydos play in the 2022 FIFA World Cup?

Hassan plays for the Qatar national team, which has qualified for the FIFA World Cup 2022 and is also the World Cup host. Hassan was included in the Qatar national team’s 26-man squad when coach Felix Sanchez revealed the team’s 26-man roster. He will lead the Qatar national team as captain.
