Inside Love Island star Molly Marshs incredible family farm where she makes TikToks for 600k foll

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 2, 2024

LOVE Island star Molly Marsh has given a glimpse inside her amazing family home.

The musical theatre performer, 21 - who is one of the sexy singletons due to take part in the upcoming summer series of the ITV2 show - will soon swap her pad for the iconic villa.

Yorkshire lass Molly loves to share videos on her TikTok which have given fans a sneak peek inside her pad she shares with her mum, dad and sister Maisie.

The siblings enjoy creating dance routines and sharing the clips on the social media platform.

Recently they showed off their moves outside their modern home, which boasts an incredible arch window to look out onto the garden.

The dining area features a brick wall, flat screen TV placed in the middle and a table and bench to sit down on.

Their hallway features in a number of Molly's videos.

It has an impressive imperial staircase, lots of natural light, plain white walls and wooden flooring.

Molly has appeared in theatre productions across the UK, including roles in national tours of the musical Oliver! and the pantomime Sleeping Beauty.

She isn't the only famous one in the family, as her mum Janet Marsh is known for her TV work and has appeared on shows like Coronation Street.

The beauty was revealed as one of this year's summer Love Islanders earlier this week.

The much-loved programme returns to our screens next week - and Molly is keen to find The One.

Speaking about going on the show, she told ITV: "I think it’s ideal timing and the perfect way to find someone.

"I don’t go out and party and I don’t use dating apps, I’m actually quite old-fashioned, so there’s no better way to meet someone than by going into a Villa in the sun with the potential ‘one’ in there.

"With my job being in social media, I’ve had some amazing opportunities to go on press trips and I’ve actually been out to the Love Island Villas in Mallorca and South Africa.

"Having the experience to walk around both was amazing but to now actually live in the Villa is going to be incredible."
