Lori Harvey rocks glitter see-through crop top and skirt at Miami bash to promote her racy swimwear

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

MODEL Lori Harvey has been living it up in Miami with a variety of stunning outfits while promoting Yevrah Swim.

The daughter of Steve Harvey recently rocked a dazzling ensemble while hosting a brunch at Art Basel.

The event took place at Casadonna Miami last week, right as the model officially opened her swimwear brand's pop-up store in the popular city.

Lori, 26, was seen posing around a decorated table while wearing a matching sequin set.

The champagne outfit consisted of a cropped tank that featured ruffles around the plunging neckline, allowing the model to show off ample cleavage.

The low-rise skirt fell just under her belly button, showing off her toned abs as it accentuated her hips.

The sparkling ensemble was paired with a belly chain, a few necklaces, and gold earrings.

The businesswoman had her curly, shoulder-length hair left down and rocked a neutral face of makeup.

Lori smiled wide as she celebrated her swimwear line, Yevrah, which was originally launched over the summer.

The 26-year-old headed to Miami Beach last week to run the opening party of her brand's new pop-up shop.

Lori wore a pink, skintight dress that went up high to her neck and fell to her mid-calf.

The figure-hugging dress had a large pink flower pattern that expanded over her chest.

Her dark hair was curled and slicked back into an updo, and she completed the look with sky-high stiletto heels and statement gold jewelry.

She was seen posing in front of a truck parked on the side of the road, which opened up and revealed racks of bikinis for sale.

Lori held her original launch event for Yevrah over the summer, where she celebrated the newest collaboration between her swimwear line and fashion brand Revolve.

The event took place at a venue with a luxurious inground pool and outdoor cabanas, which many attendees surrounded.

Lori attended the occasion with her ex-boyfriend Damson Idris, roughly three months before they announced their split in November after a year of dating.

The couple was first spotted together last December at a restaurant in West Hollywood, California, making their relationship IG official at the beginning of 2023.

LORI AND DAMSON SPLIT

Last month, it was revealed that Lori and the British actor had broken up and unfollowed each other on social media.

The ex-couple stated to The Hollywood Reporter: “We are at a point in our lives where our individual paths require our full attention and dedication.

"We part ways remaining friends with nothing but love and respect for each other and the time we shared together.”

Before the announcement, it was noted that Lori and the Snowfall actor arrived separately at Odell Beckham Jr's birthday party earlier that week.

Shortly after, internet sleuths realized the couple unfollowed each other on Instagram.
