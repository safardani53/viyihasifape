Photos Of The Couple Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

George and Amal Clooney’s love story is a timeless fairytale.

Once known as Hollywood’s most sought-after bachelor, George met the stunning international human rights lawyer, Amal Alamuddin, in a chance encounter in the summer of 2013 in the picturesque town of Lake Como, Italy. Then ended up getting dinner with his parents, and found a deep connection.

The busy duo were long-distance before their engagement. George and Amal would wed in Italy in September of 2014. Amal gave birth to the couple’s twins Ella and Alexander on June 6, 2017.

Talking about his love in 2015. “She’s an amazing human being. And she’s caring. And she also happens to be one of the smartest people I’ve ever met. And she’s got a great sense of humor.”

George and Amal are no doubt a picture-perfect couple. Keep scrolling through the gallery to see photos of them through the years.
