Kylie Minogue launches new 9 rose wine and it's on sale in Tesco

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

YOU should be so lucky, to grab a bottle of Kylie's new wine this weekend.

Well your luck is in, as Kylie Minoque has released a new rosé wine and it's available at Tesco.

Kylie Minogue's wine will set you back £9, and we can't wait to get our hands on bottle.

  • Kylie Minoque Rosé Wine, £9, Tesco - buy here

Described as "Delicate and fruity with alluring aromas of crisp summer berries and blossom", it's been made from "perfectly ripe, sun-drenched grapes from south facing vineyards".

The French rosé is described as  "delicious on its own or pairs beautifully with salads and seafood".

It's a provence style wine, that is a blend " 80% Carignan and 20% Cabernet Sauvignon from Southern France", and is reported to be made Paul Schaafsma from Benchmark Drinks - the mastermind behind other celebrity wine brands.

According to Drinks Retailing News, it's already been a huge hit with shoppers with early trial listings having proved to be popular.

The launch also coincide's with the superstar's 51st birthday - and what a way to celebrate.

The wine is available to buy in Tesco now and online, but don't hang about as we're expecting this to fly off the shelves.

Drink Retailing News also reports that "Additional wines are set to follow under the Kylie brand name: a Sauvignon Blanc and a Merlot, with a Chardonnay from western Australia to follow later in the year".

We can't wait to get our hands on a bottle.
