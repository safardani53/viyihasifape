Who was June Blair and what was her cause of death?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 1, 2024

THE ACTRESS and model was a popular figure in the public eye throughout the 1950s and 1960s.

June Blair has died at the age of 90.

Who was June Blair?

June was born Margaret June Blair on October 20, 1933, in San Francisco, California.

She was an actress and model who starred in the TV series, The Adventures of Ozzie and Harriet.

The sitcom premiered in 1952 and ran for 14 seasons.

As Variety notes, June played multiple roles on the show before being cast as Mrs June Nelson during season ten.

June was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month in January 1957.

She married her The Adventures of Ozzie and Harriet co-star, David Nelson, in 1961.

According to Variety, the pair met while working on the Western show, Two Faces West.

June played the character Julie Greer in the series, which premiered in 1960 and ran until 1961.

June and David had two children together: Daniel Blair Nelson, 60, and James Eric Nelson, 56, before divorcing in 1975.

They also have a granddaughter, Paige Margaret Nelson.

David died on January 11, 2011, at the age of 74.

What was June Blair's cause of death?

Fox News reported that June's daughter-in-law, Susan Nelson, told the publication that June died on December 5, 2022, "from natural causes" at her Sherman Oaks home in Los Angeles, California.

Nelson also told the outlet: "She was an incredible mom, grandmother, friend and devoted animal advocate, caring for all of Godâ€™s creatures with her whole heart."

She continued: "The family thanks her wonderful, devoted fans. She treasured them."

What were June Blair's other acting roles?

In addition to The Adventures of Ozzie and Harriet and Two Faces West, June had a number of acting roles.

She played the character Paula in the 1957 film noir, Hell Bound.
