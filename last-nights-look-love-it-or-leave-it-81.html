Love It or Leave It?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

GISELE BÜNDCHEN

On our to-do list this weekend: Buy whatever vitamins takes. The supermodel flaunts her famous figure in a Colicci mini featuring a plunging neckline and asymmetrical hemline backstage at the label’s show in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

JESSICA ALBA

Proving that black opaque tights let you get away with the shortest of hemlines, attends a launch dinner for Kenzo Kalifornia in a palm tree-printed embroidered shift by the label, plus chunky black booties in L.A.

MARIA MENOUNOS

isn’t the only star loving winter white. Maria steps out in a stunning Marchesa gown with a cowl-back and organza flower detail, plus eye-catching State Room earrings at the Oceana Partners Awards Gala in Beverly Hills.

FERGIE

styles a black blazer and wide-leg leather pants with a printed top and mint green cap-toe heels at the Kenzo Kalifornia launch dinner in L.A.

NAOMI WATTS

The last time the hit the carpet she was all covered up. But for a screening of Diana, she flashes way more skin in a navy floral-print Michael Kors gown with sexy cutout detail and accessorizes with a red Roger Vivier clutch and Harry Winston diamonds in N.Y.C.

KIM KARDASHIAN

Yep, she’s definitely back. The continues to flaunt her figure in a see-through lace Dolce & Gabbana dress – which reveals her black bra and hot pants – for an appearance on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno in L.A.

MOLLY SIMS

If lace look is a little too sexy for you, check out Molly’s take on the trend. She wears a long-sleeve top with skin-tight pants featuring a strip of coordinating lace down the side at a party for Stella McCartney in L.A.

JAMIE CHUNG

Doing our Celeb Style Council proud, Jamie steps out in a white strapless peplum V-neck top and pants, plus a white studded Rebecca Minkoff clutch and super-straight strands at a screening of Diana in N.Y.C.

KELLY OSBOURNE

The Fashion Police host suits up in a printed blazer, high-neck blouse with bow detail and black trousers at a party for Stella McCartney in L.A. Bonus points for that killer Chanel bag and brilliant green statement ring.

ALLISON WILLIAMS

We imagine Allison had a hard timing parting with her orange topper at the coat check. She wears the bold design over a white sleeveless top and loose pants, and accessorizes with an printed bag (all Calvin Klein Collection) at a L’Oréal Paris lunch in N.Y.C.

ALICIA SILVERSTONE

As if we’d have anything but nice things to say about Alicia’s short-sleeve LBD featuring an asymmetrical ruffled hemline at the premiere of Ass Backwards in L.A.

CAT DEELEY

Another Celeb Style Council hits the red carpet in a sparkly black mini dress and some seriously good hair at the Global Fashion Awards in London.

NICOLE RICHIE

We hope has a Tide To Go pen stashed in her clutch. She wears an all-white look including a cropped blazer, silk blouse, jeans and coordinating heels at a Chloé fashion show in L.A.

BLAKE LIVELY

To announce her major beauty news in Paris, chooses a blue-and-white off-the-shoulder Balmain mini with a flouncy pleated skirt and gold chain-link trim.

OLIVIA WILDE

No visible baby bump yet. wears a fitted Cushnie et Ochs printed dress with a Clelia clutch at the IWMF Journalism Awards in Beverly Hills.

RACHEL BILSON

Perhaps she’s testing out her “high-fashion prairie girl” Halloween costume? Rachel pairs a buttoned-up white blouse with a floor-length striped skirt and super-long locks at the Chloé fashion show in L.A.

ZOE SALDANA

She may be PEOPLE’s jean queen but opts to dress up for a charity dinner in Beverly Hills, selecting a textured blue Chloé dress featuring an asymmetrical hemline and an embellished V-neck.

ASHLEY TISDALE

Yet another look we’d love to borrow from ‘s closet. This time it’s a long printed jacket worn over a gray top and black skinnies at the Lucky Brand store opening in Beverly Hills.

ALESSANDRA AMBROSIO

Can she look any more chic? We think not. The supermodel styles a champagne silk blouse with high-waisted black trousers and nude pointy pumps (shoutout to her red lip!) at the Chloé fashion show in Beverly Hills.

GWYNETH PALTROW

Proof she’s not a fan of Halloween candy, the shows off her insanely toned abs in a crop top and black pants at a dinner to celebrate The Conversation in London.

KATY PERRY

Never one to bore us with her style, the wears a bright printed cape-like coat (which hides a two-piece look) with white peep-toe lace-up booties and a chunky gold necklace at the Sydney Opera House.

CIARA

It’s hard to stand out next to a 15-carat rock but Ciara manages just fine in an Alon Livne sheer gown featuring cutouts, patent leather detail and a mega slit at the Moet Rose Lounge in Houston.

SOFIA VERGARA

Leather pants were pretty much made to be worn by Sofia. She styles her second-skin pair with a black blazer, studded Valentino heels and a matching clutch, plus red lips and tousled waves at a Modern Family event in L.A.

KATE BOSWORTH

We love her street style, but also knows her way around an evening gown. She selects a black embellished halter dress by Christopher Kane and Ana Khouri jewels (including an ear cuff) for the premiere of Big Sur in N.Y.C.

ABIGAIL BRESLIN

Raise your hand if you were wearing bebe when you were 17. The actress selects a black Halston Heritage gown with an open back and embroidered sleeves, and accessorizes with green stone drop earrings at the premiere of Ender’s Game in L.A.

HAILEE STEINFELD

Another young star with an enviable wardrobe, Hailee chooses a one-shoulder top with a sparkly red lip print, plus skinny black pants (both by Saint Laurent) and a W. Britt golden bracelet at the premiere of Ender’s Game in L.A.

JULIE BOWEN

Joining her co-stars at the Modern Family event, the actress opts for a dramatic printed Etro dress with a flared hemline and accessorizes with an orange clutch and super-strappy sandals in L.A.

VIOLA DAVIS

Maybe Viola knew the carpet and backdrop would be black when she selected this sheath with its vibrant two-tone blue graphic at the premiere of Ender’s Game in L.A.

NATALIE PORTMAN

Three cities, three Dior looks (see here and here). This time, chooses a strapless white organza gown with a black waistband for the Berlin premiere of Thor: The Dark World.

KIM KARDASHIAN

puts her assets on display – and don’t just mean her 15-carat ring – in a cleavage-baring white lace top and matching skirt at her birthday celebration in Las Vegas.

JENNIFER HUDSON

The only thing shorter than black V-neck Balmain mini (worn with lace-up Christian Louboutin boots at BET s Black Girls Rock event in Newark, N.J.) is her newly cropped do.

ANNALYNNE MCCORD

Also showing off a new look, AnnaLynne teams her dark brown curls with a flirty strapless red Jill Stuart dress, Christian Louboutin peep-toe heels and loads of Van Cleef & Arpels diamonds at a party for the luxe jewelry brand in Santa Ana, Calif.

KELLY ROWLAND

Our Celeb Style Council member gets decked out in a Alon Livné jumpsuit (featuring a jeweled black top and sheer pants), plus statement earrings and a multi-colored mani for BET s Black Girls Rock event in Newark, N.J.

CAMILLA BELLE

The actress debuts her super-blunt bangs in an aqua Oscar de la Renta dress with floral embroidery, gold peep-toe pumps and Van Cleef & Arpels bracelets at a party for the jewelry brand in Santa Ana, Calif.

MARIAH CAREY

Sex-ay! The flashes her cleavage and abs in a black halter crop top and floor-length black-and-gold skirt (both by Alexander McQueen) at BET s Black Girls Rock event in Newark, N.J.
