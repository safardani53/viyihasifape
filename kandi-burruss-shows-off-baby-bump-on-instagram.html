Kandi Burruss Shows Off Baby Bump on Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

Kandi Burruss be baby bumpin, folks.

In a new set of Instagram photos, The Real Housewives of Atlanta cast member – who announced in July that she’s pregnant with her second child – stands proudly alongside husband Todd Tucker and shows off her evolving figure.

Along with some cute baby emojis, Kandi simply used the hashtags "#BabyBump" and "#BabyTucker" as captions for this collage.

Burruss and Tucker don’t know the gender of their impending baby just yet, but they do know he or she will turn their family of four into a family of five when he or she arrives.

Kandi has a daughter from a previous relationship, Riley; while Todd also has a daughter, Kaela.

Burruss has been open about her need to use a series of IVF treatments and hormones in order to get pregnant again, but we can confirm she is in great health at the moment.
