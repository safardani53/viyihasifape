Stassi Schroeder Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 1, 2024

Stassi Schroeder began her career as an actress and first appeared on the reality game show, The Amazing Race, together with her father Mark, her step-mother, Char, and her brother Hunter in 2005.

After some days of completion of the show Schroeder's house was covered and destroyed by Hurricane Katrina. Then, her family started living with another family, before rebuilding their house.

Stassi also worked as the Model for several years and appeared in the magazines like Italian Vanity Fair, 944, and US Weekly. Following she appeared on Queen Bees TV series, Vanderpump Rules, and The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

Talking about her social media, Schroeder is active on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. She has 333K followers on her Twitter account and 1M million on her Instagram account.

Looking at her personal life, Stassi was dating her boyfriend Patrick Meagher. After engagement currently, the couple lived together, however, once they separated and Patched up again, it seems they are planning to marry soon.

Being a TV personality, Schroeder has appeared in various reality shows from which she collects a big chunk of money. As of 2022, her estimated net worth is $2 million through which she finances her living.
