Who is Sunny Anderson? | The US Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

SUNNY Anderson has had a remarkable change of careers from serving in the Air Force to serving good food to viewers.

She has firmly established herself as a notable personality on the Food Network in recent years.

Who is Sunny Anderson?

Sunny Anderson was born on April 9, 1975, in Lawton, Oklahoma.

Sunny is a popular Food Network personality and is the host of the  Cooking for Real and Home Made in America.

She began her career on Food Network by hosting How'd That Get On My Plate? in 2008.

What did Sunny do before joining the Food Network?

Sunny Anderson was a former student of Madison High School in San Antonio, Texas. She joined the US Air Force in June 1993 after she finished high school.

She also served as a military radio host between 1993 and 1997.

After she was honorably discharged from the Air Force in 1997, she attended Loyola University in New Orleans.

Subsequently, she settled in New York City, New York, in 2001 and worked as a radio personality for HOT 97 from 2001 to 2003.

From 2003 to 2005, she briefly owned a catering company named Sunny's Delicious Dishes.

In 2005, she first appeared in Food Network as a guest on the Emeril Live program. Subsequently, she got a chance to host How'd That Get On My Plate? in 2008.

What is Sunny Anderson's net worth?

As of April 2022, Sunny Anderson has an estimated net worth of about $5million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
