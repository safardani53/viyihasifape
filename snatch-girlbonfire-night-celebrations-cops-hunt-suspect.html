Man 'tried to snatch' girl, 4, at Bonfire Night celebrations as cops hunt suspect

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 25, 2024

DETECTIVES are appealing for witnesses after a man attempted to abduct a little girl on Bonfire Night.

A man reportedly approached the four-year-old at the event on Plymouth Hoe on November 5 before trying to walk off with her.

The incident took place close to Elliot Terrace around 8pm, with officers keeping an “open mind” as to what might have happened.

Cops have now launched a manhunt for the suspect, who is described as a thin Asian man with dark hair.

A Devon and Cornwall Police spokesman said: “This happened near to Elliot Terrace on Plymouth Hoe and occurred sometime between 7:50 – 8:15pm.

“The man is described as Asian, thin build, approximately 5”8 tall, 25-30 years old.

"He had dark hair that was shaved on the sides but was longer and gelled back on top; He was wearing a black leather jacket at the time.

“The child he approached is also Asian so we are currently keeping an open mind on if this was an innocent mistake and not something more sinister.
