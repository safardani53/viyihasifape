When Emeka was a child, he moved to Bartlesville, Oklahoma, with his whole family. It was because Emeka’s father worked for Phillips Petroleum Company, and the headquarters was in Bartlesville.

As a child, Emeka loved playing basketball. That is why his father used to take him to the YMCA to learn basketball.
