KELLY Clarkson has stunned fans by wearing a formfitting holiday-themed outfit during a holiday show on NBC.

The 41-year-old sang Santa Claus is Coming to Town with the iconic Wynonna Judd, 59.

The duo opened the show for Christmas at the Opry - which is a Christmas special that aired on NBC featuring the best musicians in country music.

Kelly was wearing a stunning and shimmering emerald green dress that flowed from her waist.

The dress featured see-through long sleeves that had the same shimmer as the rest of her dress.

It also had an incredibly deep plunging neckline that was being held together by two small buttons that looked like they would pop at any moment.
