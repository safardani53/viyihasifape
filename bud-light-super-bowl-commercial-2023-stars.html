Who stars in the Bud Light Super Bowl 2023 commercial?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, February 14, 2024

SUPER Bowl LVII is right around the corner and football fans are already getting a look at the iconic commercials.

Bud Light released a clip of their commercial on February 2, 2023, causing many to wonder who the stars are.

Who stars in the Bud Light Super Bowl 2023 commercial?

The 2023 Bud Light Super Bowl commercial stars off zoomed in on a woman who has been on hold with customer service for almost an hour.

"The estimated hold time is now less than 96 minutes," the automated message says.

The commercial then zooms out and shows her husband heading to the fridge to grab two cold cans of Bud Light.

After opening the cans, her husband breaks into dance and convinces her to join him while waiting.

The words "Easy to drink, easy to enjoy," then appear on the screen, showing just how fun waiting can be with a can of Bud Light.

The stars of this commercial are Top Gun: Maverick actor Miles Teller and his wife of three years Keleigh Sperry.

"It's really been an incredible year for both Keleigh and I, not just professionally but personally," Teller told PEOPLE.

"So, when Bud Light came and wanted us to be a part of a Super Bowl commercial, I mean, that's the top of the mountain as far as these things go. We always look forward to watching them."

While the moves might have looked rehearsed, Teller revealed that there's "a lot of improv."

"We did go to a rehearsal, just to be professional about it — but in the back of my head I was kind of figuring… I kind of knew the moves I wanted to do," he continued.

Teller and Sperry tied the knot in 2019 but for the most part have kept their relationship out of the public eye, which is why fans were eager to get an inside look at their home life.

How much are Super Bowl LVII commercials?

The Super Bowl is arguably one of the biggest sporting events of the year with millions tuning in from across the country.

The big game even has something for non-football fans- iconic commercials featuring today's biggest stars as well as an over-the-top half-time show performance.

Due to the popularity, ad space during the game is quite costly and has only continued to increase as time goes on.

For the 2023 game, FOX is reportedly selling 30-second ad sports for $7million, according to Front Office Sports.

This is roughly a $1.4million increase from 2020, according to Yahoo!.

Past prices for 30-second ads include:

  • 1967- $42,500
  • 1970- $78,200
  • 1975- $107,000
  • 1980- $222,000
  • 1985- $525,000
  • 1990- $1.15million
  • 2000- $2.1million
  • 2005- $2.4million
  • 2010- $2.95million
  • 2015- $4.24million
  • 2020- $5.6million
