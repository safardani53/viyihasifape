Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 19, 2024

JEREMY Allen White has stripped down to just his underwear for a hot new Calvin Klein ad.

The Brooklyn native reached worldwide fame in 2022 for his portrayal as chef Carmen "Carmy" Berzatto in the comedy-drama series The Bear.

Now Jeremy, 32, has left fans in a tizzy after posing as the new spokesmodel for Calvin Klein Underwear.

The actor was photographed for a sexy photoshoot in New York City, where he showed off his incredible abs in the brand's classic briefs.

Calvin Klein's official Instagram page shared the campaign, starting with a black and white image of White narrowly avoiding a wardrobe malfunction on camera.

In the seductive shot, the Shameless alum flexed his flawless physique in just a pair of black briefs.

Jeremy gave the camera a sultry look as he pulled down the right side of the undergarment, exposing his right hip.

A second snap depicted the seasoned star lounging on a bright orange couch with a scene view of the NYC skyline in the backdrop.

The TV star draped one arm over his face to shield from the sun, while pulling down a pair of jeans with the other.

Jeremy's white Calvin Klein briefs were again exposed as well as his washboard abs for the sexy snap.

The brand captioned the first post: "@jeremyallenwhitefinally is iconic in Calvin Klein Underwear. Designed in New York City. Photographed by @mertalas."

The images had fans beside themselves, with one commenting that they were "hyperventilating" after seeing the post.

Calvin Klein also shared a video of the actor walking the streets of the city, ripping off a white tank top and black shorts to reveal his svelt body.

In a final series of photos, Jeremy posed against a white background.

The Iron Claw star sported the classic white brief again, but this time with a matching white t-shirt.

The actor pulled aggressively at the top while giving the camera a seductive glance.

In a second black and white image, Jeremy lay on a piece of steel overlooking Manhattan.

A final shot showed The Bear star pulling on a pair of jeans while shooting the camera an angsty expression.

THE IRON CLAW

Jeremy Allen White piled on an incredible 40 pounds of muscle for his new wrestling movie The Iron Claw.

The Hollywood actor claimed that his epic body transformation to play Kerry Von Erich was a "gross" experience. 

As well as undergoing an intense training regime, White constantly consumed calories to gain weight and hated every second of it.

"Eating all the time. Like, never stopping," he told Esquire in May. 

"In the morning, I would have waffles, almond butter. 

"In the middle of the day, I was eating turkey patties and avocado all the time. 

"It's really just gross."

By contrast, White trained in a Michelin-star restaurant to play chef Carmy in the hit FX drama The Bear.

"You're trying to consume as much as you possibly can, and, to be honest, you don't feel great," he added.
