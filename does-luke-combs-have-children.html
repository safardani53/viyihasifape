Does Luke Combs have children?

As of August 2023, the pair share two children together.

Who is Luke Combs?

Combs is a country singer and songwriter, born and raised in North Carolina.

He began performing at a young age and moved to Nashville, Tennessee to pursue his music career.

Combs' music has led him to two Grammy Award nominations, two iHeart Radio music awards, four Academy of Country Music Awards, and six Country Music Associate Awards, including the 2021 Entertainer of the Year Award.

He met his wife, Nicole, in early 2016 and they became engaged in November 2018.

What are some of Luke Combs' greatest songs?

Combs' two main studio albums are This One's for You (2017) and What You See Is What You Get (2019).

He also released a debut extended play called The Way She Rides in 2014.

Combs' biggest hits include Hurricane, Cold as You, 1,2 Many, I Got Away with You, Lovin' On You, and Angels Workin' Overtime.
