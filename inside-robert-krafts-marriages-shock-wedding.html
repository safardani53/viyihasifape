Inside Robert Kraft's marriages as shock wedding to second wife revealed

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 10, 2024

EVEN at the age of 81, the love life of businessman Robert Kraft continues to garner headlines from sports and showbiz fans alike.

This week, it was revealed that the New England Patriots owner had tied the knot with his second wife Dana Blumberg at a star-studded event in New York City.

The A-list guest list included the likes of former Patriots stars Tom Brady, Drew Bledsoe, Randy Moss, Ty Law, Vince Wilfork.

Page Six reported that Elton John performed at the event as well as Ed Sheeran.

And that’s not to mention Jon Bon Jovi, Kenny Chesney, and NBA Commissioner Adam Silver who were reportedly on hand as well.

Kraft, 81, and Dana, 47, invited guests to a “Kickoff and a Touchdown” party at the Hall des Lumières in lower Manhattan but failed to tell them that it was their big day.

For Kraft, who is estimated to be worth $10.6 billion, it wasn’t his first rodeo having once tied the knot with his school sweetheart Myra Hiatt.

The pair were married for nearly 50 years before Myra tragically died of ovarian cancer in 2011.

From then on his love life took its ups and down including his high profile romance with actress Ricki Lander.

Here’s everything you need to know about his marriages about previous relationships.

Myra Hiatt

In June 1963, Kraft married Myra - a 1964 graduate of Brandeis University.

As the daughter of the late businessman and philanthropist Jacob Hiatt, she served on boards and contributed several national and international charities.

According to an obituary she also served as chairwoman of the Combined Jewish Philanthropie - with her father a survivor of the Holocaust.

Together Robert and Myra shared four sons named Jonathan, Daniel, Joshua, and David.

“I sort of feel robbed,” Kraft admitted after her death. “I try to stay very busy, I basically work seven days a week. I try to do new things, to meet new people.”

Riki Lander

A year after her passing, Robert embarked on a fresh chance at romance with actress Ricki Lander.

In the early stages of their relationship, the businessman helped Lander with an audition tape for a role in a new movie.

With Lander in a bikini, Kraft gives her a hand by reciting lines, doing some dancing, and even throwing a punch after uttering a few lines.

After that the couple they tended to keep the nature of their relationship private until they eventually split in 2018.

According to sources at the Daily Mail, the pair remained friends and on good terms.

Dana Blumberg

In 2019, a year later, Kraft began dating Dr. Dana Blumberg, one of the foremost ophthalmologists in the country.

The pair were first spotted together at the French Open and a month later at the FIFA Women’s World Cup finals in Paris.
