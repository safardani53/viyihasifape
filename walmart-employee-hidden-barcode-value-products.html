I'm a Walmart employee and there's a hidden barcode in most Great Value products - but no one knows

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 6, 2024

A WALMART employee has revealed a hidden barcode inside most of their Great Value products that no one knew about.

TikTok user, Aidan Bost, uploaded a hack for workers who work in the OPD industry (Online Pickup and Delivery).

This simple step may save time for those who are delivering online orders.

In the short video, the content creator is scanning Great Value products and pricing them for their online customers.

Aidan Bost captioned the video: "I found a shortcut."

In his comment section of 550 people, many of the comments were from people claiming to be former employees, who bonded over the tip.

One comment read: "I work in Walmart ogp/opd this is real, but it doesn't work on all great value products."

Another person said: "I thought I was insane when I scanned the front of the box, and it worked."
