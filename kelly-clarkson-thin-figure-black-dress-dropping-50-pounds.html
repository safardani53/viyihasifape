Kelly Clarkson shows off thinner-than-ever figure in sparkly black dress on show after 'dropping 50

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

KELLY Clarkson has shown off her major weight loss in a black sparkly dress.

The singer recently lost 50 pounds in eight months.

In a preview for an upcoming episode, Kelly, 41, flaunted her fit figure.

"It's Jason Momoa, everybody!" she said in the clip as the actor walked out from behind stage.

Jason, 44, is set to appear on The Kelly Clarkson Show on Monday, December 18.

In the video, Kelly wore a figure-hugging black dress.

After seven years of marriage, Kelly split from her ex-husband Brandon Blackstock, 47, in 2020.

O-MY!

An expert told The U.S. Sun recently that Kelly lost 50 pounds in eight months.

The talk show host has not publicly talked about how much weight she currently lost.

However, in 2018, she said that she lost 37 pounds.

The U.S. Sun spoke to a physician who specializes in nutritional medicine about Kelly's weight loss.

Comparing photos from March 2023 at the iHeartRadio Music Awards and photos from November 2023 at a SiriusXM event, Dr. Fred Pescatore believes Kelly lost 50 pounds.

"I think she has lost close to 50 pounds," he told The U.S. Sun.

Pescatore noticed a significant change in her "hands, face and her breast size," just from looking at the photos.

Certain body parts are not visible in the photos as Kelly was fully-clothed.

While Kelly has not admitted to using Ozempic or any similar weight loss drugs, the doctor, who has not treated nor has any ties to Kelly Clarkson, thinks she might have used it.

"It is possible she is on a diet, but all things point to Ozempic or Mounjaro," he claimed.
