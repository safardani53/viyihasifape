Who is Daisy Keech? | The US Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 18, 2024

DAISY Keech's online popularity led to the formation of Hype House and other web-based collectives.

Aside from her time with her fellow Hype House influencers, fans are curious to know more about Daisy and how she curates her social media image.

Who is Daisy Keech?

Born on August 12, 1999, Daisy Keech is a vlogger and Internet personality.

Through Instagram and YouTube, she garnered a following for her fitness-themed content.

Her most popular content revolves around her 'Hourglass Abs' workout tutorials and tips on which exercises will lead to a bigger posterior.

On Instagram, Daisy amassed a following of 6million users.

On YouTube, her fanbase is made up of over 3.3million subscribers.

On TikTok, her follower count is the highest of all of her profiles with 8million users.

Through her official website, Daisy provides workout programs dedicated to growing a bigger rear-end.

Prices for her butt-focused fitness plans range from $47 to $97.

In December 2019, Daisy teamed up with Chase Hudson and Thomas Petrou to form the Los Angeles-based influencer group Hype House.

The three personalities cultivated the collective with Addison Rae and sister duo Charli and Dixie D'Amelio.

When did Daisy leave Hype House?

In February 2020, Thomas Petrou revealed that Daisy left the group sometime in the latter part of the month.

Following her departure, she and Thomas traded multiple insults towards one another and continuously disputed each other's recollection of what led to Daisy leaving.

Regarding her side of the matter, she told Seventeen: "I felt like at the Hype House, the people with the most followers, they felt like they had more authority.

"I felt very undervalued and I never ever want any of the members or any of the staff to ever feel that way because everyone has such a big role in this."

In April of that year, Daisy formed another influencer group named the Club House.

"I've learned to create a really strong internal structure because we were a thousand percent lacking that in the Hype House," she said to the above-mentioned outlet.

When did Daisy leave the Club House?

On August 4, 2020, Daisy shared with her followers that she left the Club House.

She attributed the reason behind her exit to her desire to pursue careers in interior design and home renovation.

The fitness influencer also stated that she wants to focus on her own brand and not dedicate her time to groups with several content creators.

In her YouTube video, Daisy explained: "The reason I’m moving out is that I really need to focus on myself and my own brand that I truly want to build.
