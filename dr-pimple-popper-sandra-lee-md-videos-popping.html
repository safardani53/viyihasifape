Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 14, 2024

FANS of Dr. Pimple Popper expressed disgust on Twitter as they watched a particularly nasty episode on TLC on Wednesday.

A woman with a cyst the “size of a baby’s HEAD” on her arm went to Dr. Sandra Lee for help and it clearly turned viewers' stomachs.

Among the tweets included a GIF of a man vomiting and a Twitter user who wrote: "my grandma likes to watch Dr. Pimple Popper while we eat [vomiting emoji]"

"Has anyone run across the Dr. Pimple Popper channel, I just did, and I just about lost it!" came another tweet.

Another person chimed in: "Me: *watching Dr Pimple Popper* Ror: “what are you watching Auntie? What is that gross thing?"

Giving fans a glimpse of what was to come, Sandra took to Instagram on Wednesday to share a clip for Dr. Pimple Popper’s latest episode.

The teaser began with a woman discussing her large cyst, explaining: “The bump looks like the top of a baby’s head.”

“Like I’m delivering a baby that I’ve carried around for 16 years,” she said as the camera gave a close-up look at the cyst near her shoulder.

The teaser continued with other patients explaining their problems, as one man showed off a large red mark on his skin that looks like “a geographical map.”

A following patient unzipped his jeans before showing off a skin tag, while he was heard saying: “Boy, look at that.”

Sandra, 50, then shared her thoughts on the particular mark, joking: “It was smaller than I thought it would be.”

Alongside the clip, the dermatologist wrote: “Watch my new episode Tonight! Wednesday at 9/8c on TLC👉🏼💥👈🏼”

DR. LEE'S PRICES

While Sandra’s sessions are documented on the TV show, viewers may not know much an appointment with the celebrity doctor costs.

The doctor’s starts off with a $120 consultation fee, though the price only goes up from there depending on the work needed.

According to Cheat Sheet, Sandra’s extraction procedures can range anywhere from $500 to $600 each.

Along with the hefty price tag comes a battle with insurance companies as many view professional pimple popping as an elective cosmetic procedure.

Sandra- who rose to fame by uploading grossly satisfying videos of dermatological techniques on YouTube- first began starring on Dr. Pimple Popper in 2018.

MORE CASES

During a previous episode of the TLC show, viewers got to watch as Sandra treated a man named Roger with two large rhinophymas on his nose.

Rhinophyma can be caused by rosacea, which is a thickening of oily skin.

The steelworker revealed to Sandra that the lumps started as a pimple 11 years ago.

The two huge growths on his nose attracted flies and made it difficult for him to breathe, while he explained: "I have this big old growth on my nose. They have little holes. That's where all the puss comes out.”
