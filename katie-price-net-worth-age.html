YOU'D be hard to find someone in Britain who doesn't know who Katie Price is.

However, over the years she has become more known for her money troubles and love life, than for her past glamour modelling career.

How old is Katie Price?

Katie Price is a former glamour model who started her career under the name Jordan on The Sun's Page 3 back in 1996, aged 17.

Born on May 22, 1978, she became known for her extensive boob job ops and made the most of her notoriety by cashing in where she could.

The public got to know another side to Katie when she appeared in I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here in 2004.

During this time, she decided to ditch her Jordan alter-ego and open up to the public her true self - and the UK fell in love with her.

Katie has featured in numerous TV shows including Katie & Peter, What Katie Did Next, Signed By Katie Price and My Crazy Life.

She won Celebrity Big Brother in 2015, and has also taken part in Celebrity MasterChef.

What is Katie Price's net worth?

As of 2023, Katie’s net worth now reportedly stands at less than £1million, with multiple bankruptcy hearings revealing the true state of her finances.

At the peak of her career, her involvement in fashion, television, writing and modelling saw her net worth soar to an estimated £45million.
