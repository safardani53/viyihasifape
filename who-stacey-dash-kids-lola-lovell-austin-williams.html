Who are Stacey Dash's kids?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 23, 2024

ACTRESS, author, and former Fox News commentator Stacey Dash has two children and has been married four times.

Her two kids remain out of the spotlight; however, this is what we know about them.

Who are Stacey Dash's kids?

Stacey Dash has two children, Lola Lovell, 19, and Austin Williams, 30.

In a 2020 interview with US Magazine, Stacey said: “My kids give me strength."

"They give me wisdom."

"They show me that I am worthy of this role in this life."

"They are love in action for me.”

Lola, born in 2003, is 19 now and an aspiring ballerina.

On June 21, 2019, Stacey shared a sweet birthday message to her daughter on her Instagram, telling her how blessed she was to have her.

The Clueless star called Lola a "beautiful, smart, strong and kind girl."

Lola chooses to stay quiet and private on her social media platforms.

As for Stacey's son, Austin Williams was born in 1991.

He's currently a model and is the great-nephew of jazz legend Ella Fitzgerald on his father's side.

Stacey has been extremely vocal since the release of her book There Goes My Social Life: From Clueless to Conservatism about how she nearly aborted Austin when she was just a few weeks pregnant.

Dash told People: “When I got pregnant, I was doing a lot of drugs and I didn’t want to live."

" I wanted to die."

"I was going to have an abortion."

"I was crying and I said to God, ‘Please tell me what to do.’ And God told me, ‘Keep your son.’"

"I ripped the IV out of my arm and I said, ‘I’m keeping my son.'”

Stacey also said that she had been and would always be honest with her children about her past.

She said: "The best way to protect my children is to be honest with them."

“I let them know that I survived. I’m not a victim. And there is nothing they can’t overcome.”

Who are the fathers of Stacey Dash's children?

Austin's father is singer and actor Christopher Williams, who is best known for hits like “I’m Dreaming” and “Promises, Promises."

Lola's father is producer Brian Lovell.

Brian and Stacey met in January 1999 and had a whirlwind romance.

They dated for just six months before they walked down the aisle together.

The couple split in 2006 due to disputes and complications in their relationship.

What did Stacey reveal on the Dr Oz Show?

She announced on the Dr Oz Show on Thursday, October 14, 2021, that she was celebrating five years of sobriety.

She shared that she was at one point taking 18 to 20 Vicodin pills per day to alleviate anger and "fill the hole."

The 55-year-old former 90s starlet, who played Dionne Marie Davenport in the 1995 film Clueless, has opened up about her history of addiction when she was first introduced to cocaine at 16 years old.

Stacey also revealed that her parents struggled with addiction.
