Harry's Meat and Gatsby's Fete

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 15, 2024

Kyle Richards is not someone who likes people talking behind her back, so you can only imagine how betrayed she felt to hear about Eden Sassoon branding her as Kim Richards’ enabler. 

When The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 7 Episode 10 got underway, Lisa Rinna was preparing for a barbecue when Eden showed up with a bottle for her. 

Dorit Kemsley, Eileen Davidson, Camille Grammer and Erika Girardi were not far behind. As you probably know if you watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills online, Eden has been very vocal about Kim and it’s getting to the point were nobody wants to talk to her. 

It’s one thing to point out that Kim is not doing well, but telling everyone within a 200 mile radius is just a step too far. 

"I don’t think Kim knows or cares what anyone else thinks," Eden said to Dorit, who seemed like she wanted to run away from the barbecue just to get a break from the accusations. 

"Kim’s behavior at PK’s birthday was really closed off. She didn’t listen to a word I had to say, and she didn’t care. As an addict, that’s a red flag," she said to the camera.

The rest of the barbecue was filled with Eden trying to preach to anyone who would listen to her that there was something wrong with Kim. Bravo execs, please do not make Eden a permanent housewives.

She sounds like a broken record and is seriously making me consider ditching the show. 

When Eileen commented to Kyle that she chatted with Kim at PK’s party, Eden kicked off… again. She’s like one of those distance sellers that just won’t stop trying to sell you something. 

Kim kind of had enough and was really rude to her, but you can understand why. She’s branded her Kim’s enabler, so she’s probably upset at that claim. 

Lisa R was also pretty uncomfortable with the whole thing. As the argument intensified, Camille, Eileen, Erika and Rinna left the situation. 

Kyle then decided it was best to try and shut it down. 

"I’m not upset with you for having your feelings. We all need support systems, right?"

Kyle was then practically forced into a hug that made her feel so uncomfortable. 

Later, it was time for Kyle’s party and she was nervous that Eden was going to start her crap again with her. Dorit was quick to reveal to Kyle that Eden called her an enabler.

Things got awkward when Kyle was chatting with Kim and Eden compared them to her and her dead sister. 

"Just to be clear, I am not an enabler," Kim told Eden. Eden then tried to imply that she was right, but Kim was having none of it. 

"You were looking for trouble, and I didn’t want to be around that," Kim explained.

"I just need you to back away from me a little bit." However, Eden was quick to throw Lisa Rinna under the bus, prompting Kyle to question her loyalty. 

Lisa Vanderpump showed up at the last minute to defend her BFF and her statement was pretty darn great. 

"Let me tell you my point of view: We started off on the right foot, and I’m happy about that, but my other foot will kick you in the ass if you come after my girl Kyle."
