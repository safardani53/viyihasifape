Growing up, she acquired an interest in singing, and in the late 1990s, she started her singing career by creating a group called “Sirens” with other girls from the northeast of England. She subsequently went on to compete as a competitor on the famous reality program “Popstars,” where she finished as one of the five runner-ups, prompting her to join the group “Liberty.”
