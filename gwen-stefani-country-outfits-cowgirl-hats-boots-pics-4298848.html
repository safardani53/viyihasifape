Gwen Stefanis Best Country-Inspired Outfits Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, October 7, 2024

Gwen Stefani may have stepped onto the music scene as a ska punk artist, but she definitely knows how to dress like a country singer! The talented musician, whose fiance is country singer Blake Shelton, has loved to show off different looks over the years ,and many of them have included items like cowgirl boots, cowgirl hats, and a lot of pieces with fringe! Whether she’s on stage performing, attending an event, or on a casual outing, this pretty lady sure knows how to stun in her country outfits, and we’re highlighting some of our favorites right here.

Gwen showed off her most recent country outfit on New Year’s Eve in a video post she shared to promote her performance on NBC’s special, which can be seen above. She walked toward the camera while wearing a star-shaped bikini top with a denim jacket and jeans that were draped in crystal fringe. She also topped the look off with silver slouchy boots.

In another country look, Gwen wore a long-sleeved green and black button-down top that had blue flowers and white stars on it, and black fringe with matching fishnets and bright pink cowgirl boots. She posed in the awesome outfit while standing next to Blake in a cute Instagram pic, which can be seen below, on Dec. 16. She also added a white cowgirl hat to literally top things off.

Gwen stepped out in yet another country-inspired look during an outing at a her local nail salon in L.A. in Jan. 2020. It included a plaid button-down shirt over a black graphic tee and jeans. She also wore cow print ankle boots and round sunglasses that looked great on her as she donned her freshly done long nails.

It’s always awesome to catch Gwen wearing these looks and appreciating all kinds of fashion. We’re not sure if her country style was inspired by Blake, but we’re loving the outfits either way! Check out the gallery above to see more of Gwen’s cowgirl boots, hats and more!
