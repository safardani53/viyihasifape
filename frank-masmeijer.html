Frank Masmeijer Age, Biography

Frank Masmeijer was born in Assen on 25 August 1961 and raised in Hilversum, Netherlands. His nationality is Dutch and he is of Dutch-White ancestry. In 2022, he celebrated his 61st birthday.

After completing HAVO, he spent two years at the football residential school Go Ahead Eagles in Deventer, where he earned his VWO diploma. In his teenage years, he played football for the club’s junior team. He also played amateur football for FC Hilversum. In 1984, he moved to Mallorca and worked as a hotel sports entertainer for seven months.

He concluded his secondary education at a local Deventer, Netherlands, high school.
