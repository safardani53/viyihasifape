The Handmaids Tale Season 5 Is Set To Be Released On Hulu Soon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, March 16, 2024

The Handmaid’s Tale returns to Hulu with its fifth season, and viewers will finally get to see what happened to June Osborne following the events of the previous season. The drama takes place in the fictitious state of Gilead, a dystopia for women in which they are repressed and forced into reproductive responsibilities by a religious organization. The narrative follows June, a handmaid who struggles against persecution and attempts to rescue herself and her sisters.

Season 5 will continue off where Season 4 left off, with June viciously murdering her abuser, Commander Fred Waterford, and being brought by Moira to safely escape the border. June’s emotional choice has put her in a difficult situation. Everyone is wondering, “What will happen to her next?”

The release date for The Handmaid’s Tale season 5 has been investigated.

Season 5 of The Handmaid’s Tale will premiere on Hulu on September 14, 2022. Hulu will broadcast the first two episodes on the specified date, with the remaining episodes following in weekly installments. The following is the release schedule for the fifth season of the show:

  • The first episode of Season 5 will air on September 14, 2022.
  • The second episode of Season 5 will air on September 14, 2022.
  • Episode 3 of Season 5 will air on September 21, 2022.
  • Episode 4 of Season 5 will air on September 28, 2022.
  • 5th episode of Season 5: October 5, 2022.
  • Episode 6 of Season 5 will air on October 12, 2022.
  • Episode 7 of Season 5 will air on October 19, 2022.
  • Episode 8 of Season 5 will air on October 26, 2022.

Remember to watch Hulu every Wednesday for the next seven weeks to see new episodes of The Handmaid’s Tale season 5. New episodes are released at 12 a.m. ET or 3 a.m. ET on the streaming platform.

What can we anticipate from Season 5?

Season 5 will include a battle between June Osborne and Serena Waterford, Fred Waterford’s devastated widow. Serena is preparing her vengeance after her husband’s murder last season. The season 5 summary is as follows:

“June is dealing with the fallout from murdering Commander Waterford while also attempting to reestablish her identity and purpose. Serena, a widow, strives to boost her profile in Toronto as Gilead’s influence spreads. Commander Lawrence collaborates with Aunt Lydia in his efforts to rebuild Gilead and climb to power. June, Luke, and Moira continue their attempt to rescue and reconnect with Hannah while fighting Gilead from afar.”

Showrunner Bruce Miller adapted Margaret Atwood’s best-selling book The Handmaid’s Tale for television. Since its debut on Hulu in 2017, the series has been one of the most-watched. Nina Fiore, John Herrera, Kim Todd, Joseph Boccia, and Lisa Clapperton produce the show.

The Handmaid’s Tale has received a number of accolades, including the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Series and the Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series – Drama. It’s still one of Hulu’s most-watched series, and season 5 promises to keep viewers delighted. On September 14, 2022, the fifth chapter of the dystopian drama series will be released.
