Inside Kim Kardashian's son's over-the-top 7th birthday bash at LA Rams game with football cake &amp; '$

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 7, 2024

KIM Kardashian has given fans an inside look at her son Saint's extravagant 7th birthday bash at the Los Angeles Rams game.

The Hulu star showed off the party's $60,000 venue and the birthday boy's football-shaped cake as the Rams played the Seattle Seahawks.

Kim, 42, shared snapshots of several fun-filled moments from the seven-year-old's party on her Instagram Monday.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum captioned the post: "Happy 7th Birthday to my baby boy. I love you so so so much! I love seeing you grow into the kindest soul ever. Mommy loves you forever and ever!"

The first photo captured Kim and Saint standing on the football field as they posed near one of the end zones.

Saint donned a Jalen Ramsey jersey for the day's events while Kim kept it casual in a black zip-up track jacket with matching loose-fitting pants.

The next shot showed Saint carrying a football along the sidelines as his friends, who were also dressed in Ram's gear, played close by.

The seven-year-old was then depicted holding the Ram's 2022 Super Bowl trophy as the stands start to fill up around him.

Saint was shown screaming with delight as he attempts to lift the Lombardi Trophy.

In the following post, Kim's oldest son is photographed rocking the diamond-encrusted jewelry that comes along with winning the big game.

He is shown staring down at his hand, mesmerized by the gigantic ring on his finger.

Saint is later pictured inside SoFi stadium's VIP suite with the rest of his similarly-aged guests.

In one photo he again holds the Lombardi trophy while in another he and his friends excitedly surround the Los Angeles Rams mascot, Rampage.

Kim then managed to capture a photo of the football-shaped caked before it was devoured by the kids.

"Saint" along with the number "7" was written on the cake in blue and yellow icing, as was the phrase, "Happy Birthday Saint," in front of the cake.

A pair of photos showing Kim and Saint watching the game was included near the end of the lengthy post.

The Kardashians star blocked out the sun with dark shades as she watched the action happening on the field.

The post's final entry featured a short video of Saint and his birthday guests celebrating from inside their private booth.

SLIM KIM

Kim Kardashian sparked concern with her "tiny" frame after attending the Los Angeles Rams game with her son Saint.

The TV personality star was seen posing for a group snap with Saint, seven, and Los Angeles Rams player Jalen Ramsey, 28, after a game on Sunday.

Kim stood behind Saint with her arms around his shoulders as Jalen signed a group of kids' footballs.

The reality star, who was wearing a skintight bodysuit and baggy pants, then ran around to the back of the group for a photo.

She flashed her signature pout and peace sign at the camera, as Saint stood at the front of the group with Jalen next to him.

A video of the exchange was shared on TikTok via the Los Angeles Rams' official account.

Viewers were floored by how "short" and "tiny" Kim looked in the clip.

One wrote, "Kim looks extra short here," and another commented: "Kim is so tiny in real life."

A third posted: "Wow she almost smaller than Saint at this point."

"Yeah it's so apparent in this video," yet another user agreed.
