6. AS2 Foosball Table

  • L x W x H: 56 x 31 x 36 inches
  • Weight: 310 pounds

Starting our list on number 6, we have the Atomic AS2 Foosball Table. The table features a unique head design where instead of traditional foosball men with heads, it has two feet called “hammerhead.”

The table has a two-inch thick side, sturdy cabinet walls, and dual backside ball returns. The unique hammerhead offers counterbalance and helps with new ball control skills and tricks.

Similarly, the AS2 table has durable, self-lubricating nylon bushings that provide smooth and consistent rod action. The table also features high-density rubber bumpers that add shock resistance.

It has the traditional wood 5-bead abacus-style scoring. There are leg levelers with three-inch diameter rubber pads that rotate. As a result, the playing surface is leveled and stable.

AS2 Foosball Table has octagonal wooden handles, which offer a secure grip. Furthermore, there are also recessed cup holders.

On number 5 of our list of the amazing performing Atomic foosball table is the Atomic AS1 Foosball Table. It is the lower-end version of the AS2 foosball table but with a lighter cabinet design.

The AS1 table has similar features to that of the AS1. However, the cabinet of the AS1 is less sturdy compared to the initial design; hence, it is lighter with a blonde bamboo finish.

Like the AS2, the AS1 table also features the unique “hammerhead” counterbalanced foosball men and dual backside ball returns. It has a unique gunmetal goal trim that provides a goal post effect.

The table had high-density rubber bumpers for shock resistance purposes and durable, self-lubricating nylon bushings for smooth and consistent rod action. Similarly, the table has a similar octagonal wooden foosball handle for a secure grip.

The AS2 table features leg levelers with three-inch diameter rubber pads that rotate. Furthermore, it has 4 x 4-inch solid MDF foosball legs for stability and stationary placement.

On number 4, we have the Atomic Euro Star Foosball Table. It is a durable table with 3/8-inches MDF playfield warping resistance features that make it ideal for any home, gaming room, or office.

For those who have kids and teenagers at home, the Euro Star is one of the top-rated foosball tables for them. It has a sturdy build with 5/8 inches of hollow steel rods with a chrome finish.

The table is a European-style soccer table with a black exterior and chrome accents. Similarly, it has the brand’s signature octagonal handles with soft-touch rubber for a comfortable grip.

The Atomic Gladiator Foosball Table is our third pick for the amazing performing Atomic foosball table. This beautiful table has a sleek design but is also the heaviest foosball table on our list.

The table is constructed with solid chrome 5/8 inches steel rods and features the brand’s signature octagonal wood handles for easy grip. Similarly, it has a 1/2-inch MDF playfield that resists warping.

Aside from its beautiful design, the Gladiator table’s foosball men are of black and red colors on a green playfield. The foosball men are weighted and balanced, allowing players to shoot the ball without other rods blocking the shots.

Similarly, the table has internal ball return and ball entry with abacus scoring to keep track of the scores. It has three and half-inch leg levelers for level and even game playing.

Gladiator Foosball Table has integrated cup holders. The great thing about this foosball table is that the table comes with not two but four balls. The table is a 56-inch standard foosball table and is among the highest-selling tables under $500.
