The Bold and the Beautiful fans fear Steffy is DEAD along with husband Finn after she's shot in late

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

THE Bold and the Beautiful fans are now fearing for Stephanie “Steffy” Forrester’s life, alongside her husband Finn’s, after she was shot in the latest episode.

Portrayed by Jacqueline MacInnes Wood, viewers had already been wondering if the actress was set to leave the series after she announced her pregnancy last year.

Spoilers for Monday's episode continue below.

During Friday’s episode, Steffy watched as her husband Dr. John "Finn" Finnegan (played by Tanner Novlan) was accidentally shot by his biological mother, Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown).

With the new episode, it appeared Finn’s efforts to save his wife was for nothing as Sheila still went on to shoot Steffy, who was trying to save her husband, leaving both presumably dead.

While Bold and the Beautiful fans had trouble noticing exactly where Steffy was hit, as she already had blood on her from tending to her shot hubby, many thought she took a bullet to the neck.

For the rest of the episode, both of little Hayes’ parents lay with their eyes closed, bleeding, on bags of garbage out back behind the Italiian restaurant.

Seemingly trying to make it look like a robbery gone bad, Sheila quickly checked Stef’s pulse before stealing all of her jewelry and putting on gloves to try to wipe any prints.

She looked heartbroken as she took one last look at her son Finn and gave him a goodbye kiss on the lips before she broken down in tears while running away.

Once home, she threw away her gloves and Steffy’s jewelry in the garbage and went to wash and scrub her bloody hands in the sink.

After letting what just happened fully sink in, Sheila began to cry and scream in agony as she stared at herself in the mirror.

It wasn’t until the very end of the episode that Finn and Steffy were discovered Deacon Sharpe, who was freaked out by quickly called 911 for help.

Fans were hoping that one or both of the characters would at least open their eyes or move a finger at the end of the broadcast, to give them some hope, but neither did.

Now, viewers fear both might be dead.

One angry fan tweeted: “R they both dead? Both Steffy AND Finn? Would they REALLY kill off Steffy?”

Another wrote: “Yeah, if BOTH Steffy and Finn don't end up alive and thriving, I'm never watching again”

A third furious viewer said: “They have Steffy and Finn bleeding out on trash bags and just threw #Sinn rings in the trash. 

“That’s where this show is going for me. In the trash. 

“They are literally telling me - nothing left here for you.”

Others said they are absolutely “shook” by this storyline and are very worried about “poor Steffy and Finn.”

All of this comes after Friday's cliffhanger episode.

Steffy learned that Sheila caused Stef's step-mom, Brooke, to relapse by switching out her non-alcoholic champagne with the real version.

After alerting Finn of this, Steffy told her husband she was going to confront his mom about it about it at Il Giardino.

He became increasingly worried about the mother of his child when she wouldn’t respond to his texts and decided to leave work to rush over the Italian restaurant he knew they'd be at.

Finn got there just as Sheila had raised a gun at Steffy out back by the dumpster, and he jumped in front and was shot instead.

Shocked, Steffy ran over to try to wake her unconscious husband and to try to stop all of the blood, screaming as she realized how much there was.

As she worked to dial 9-1-1 for help, Sheila once again pointed the gun at her and forbade her from finishing the call.

Fans rushed to social media after the episode to share their fear over Finn's likely death, before going on to do the same after Monday's episode for both his and Steffy's possible deaths.

Tanner, who's married to Vampire Diaries alum Kayla Ewell, has not yet spoken out about the cliffhanger ending or whether or not he's leaving the series.

Neither has Jacqueline about her status on the show.
