Celebrities React To Regina Kings Sons Death Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Hollywood is sending out their love and support to Regina King as she mourns the loss of her only child. Halle Berry, Janet Jackson, Kid Cudi, Josh Gad and more celebrities expressed sadness upon hearing the tragic news that Regina’s son Ian Alexander Jr. died by suicide at the age of 26. Many of the social media posts included heartbreaking photos of the mother and son, while others simply shared their heartfelt condolences.

“Sending my deepest condolences to my friend @iamreginaking and her entire family, following the passing of her son, Ian,” shared Halle on her Instagram, alongside a snap of Regina leaning her head on Ian. “Regina, I pray the love that you pour into others every day is sent your way, tenfold, during this time.” Janet posted the same picture and wrote, “I’m so sorry to hear about Ian. Please know that I am here for u @iamreginaking. Sending all my love, prayers and condolences,” while Kid Cudi tweeted, “My heart hurts for Regina King and her family. God please watch over them.”

The amazing support and love from Tinseltown’s best and brightest comes after Regina announced the tragic death of Ian on Saturday (January 22). “Our family is devastated at the deepest level by the loss of Ian,” Regina, 51, said in a statement, per People. “He is such a bright light who cared so deeply about the happiness of others. Our family asks for respectful consideration during this private time. Thank you.” HollywoodLife has reached out to Regina’s reps for comment.
