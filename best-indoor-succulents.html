Best Indoor Succulents 2019 | The Sun UK

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

SUCCULENTS are the perfect purchase for those who want to decorate their home with plants, as they are low maintenance.

The indoor house plants are ideal for people who have busy schedules, want to add a pop of colour into their home and purify the air in their home.

Succulents can be propped up in flats as well as houses, because there are smaller versions that do not take up much room.

There are a variety of succulents available, from aloe vera and common cactus plants, to ones suitable for hanging, as well as plants that bloom and produce a delicate flower.

But not all succulents have the same requirements, as some grow better in natural light, while others thrive in cooler environments, perhaps best left in a shaded corner of a room.

Succulents are a great way for you to be creative, as you can choose different pots to put them in to suit the decor, buy individual plants to dot around the home or a ready made selection in a pot - the options are endless.
