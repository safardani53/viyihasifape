The 13 Best Hair Growth Products of 2024

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 22, 2024

Through our own testing and extensive research, we found the best hair growth products that elongate the growth cycle, promote thicker strands, and provide deep hydration to prevent breakage. What to Look for In Hair Growth Products

Ingredients 

Formula ingredients are important in hair growth products, especially key ingredients. No matter what hair care product you use, take a look at the back of the bottle or ingredients list on the retailer's product page and review the ingredients featured. It’s best to use formulas that incorporate ingredients clinically proven to provide hair growth benefits. Dr. Peter Young, M.D., a medical director at Keeps, says to look for plant-based, organic, and science-backed ingredients like biotin, caffeine, and green tea, which “support hair growth and give the appearance of thicker, healthier-looking hair.” Nourishing and hydrating ingredients like antioxidant-rich argan oil and hyaluronic acid are also noteworthy as these can give hair a hydration boost which — like skincare — is essential for optimal scalp and hair health. 

In addition to haircare ingredients, Dr. Young says hair growth medications are also worth considering (though it is recommended that you consult a professional first). “Minoxidil is an over-the-counter medication that comes in liquid or foam form and is available at two strengths,” he notes. While the medication can take at least three to four months for results to show, the medication is a known and proven solution for hair loss. “Minoxidil helps to shorten the telogen phase of the hair cycle (the shedding phase, which results in excessive daily hair fall) which becomes longer in male or female pattern hair loss and prolongs the time follicles spend actively growing,” Kinglsey adds, noting that it’s the only FDA-approved topical medication for hair growth at the moment. 

Scalp Serum

When shopping for hair growth products, Kingsley says to consider investing in a scalp serum “to maintain [an] optimal scalp environment, stimulate follicle health, and reduce hair shedding.” Scalp serums come in a variety of formulas and the ones designed for hair growth can sometimes lead to longer hair, but can also target other concerns like hair density, breakage, and overall health. 

For a scalp serum designed to trigger hair growth, consider the Act + Acre Stem Cell Scalp Serum, which is formulated with active ingredients that have been clinically studied to elongate the hair growth phase as well as reduce shedding. Another good scalp serum for hair growth is the Divi Scalp Serum, which is included in the Divi Starter Bundle and features Copper Tripeptide-1 and other potent ingredients to also help elongate the hair growth phase and support an overall healthy scalp. 

Those who experience hair thinning might also benefit from a thickening serum such as the Vegamour GRO Hair Serum, which includes ingredients such as turmeric, red clover, and copper that work together to support hair growth as well as prevent shedding and keep thinning at bay. 

Hydration 

According to Kingsley, hydration is key when treating hair for improved growth and density. “Healthy hair will stretch up to a third of its length when wet; however, dry, damaged hair will snap under very little tension, causing breaking and inhibiting hair from growing longer and stronger,” he explains. With this in mind, hydrating ingredients and formulas can put the stretch and elasticity back into strands and result in stronger, less breakage-prone hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do hair growth products actually work?

    “It widely varies on the formulation, product type, and brand,” says Kingsley. When in doubt, he recommends looking at ingredients and formulas that undergo clinical trials with proven results. And, if you experience excessive hair loss, it’s recommended to seek the advice of a professional, who can provide further insight on the cause of your hair loss and might prescribe additional measures such as Minoxidil.

  • Can thinning hair grow back?

    While hair thinning might feel alarming, experts assure that thinning hair can grow back, especially when taking good care of your hair with clinically-proven ingredients and formulas and, in more severe cases, using over-the-counter medications such as Minoxidil. With that said, Dr. Young says it’s also important to address health habits that can compound the issue of thinning hair. 

    “Taking care of yourself with a healthy, well-balanced diet, getting plenty of sleep, and eliminating stressors in your life will help maintain proper functions of your body, including hair growth,” he explains. He recommends consulting with your healthcare provider, who might order a blood test to check for vitamin and nutrient levels: “Taking certain vitamins could curb that extra hair shedding if your levels are low.”

  • What oil is best for hair growth?

    According to Dr. Young, there is not just one oil that is best for hair growth — there are several. “There is one study that shows that coconut oil assisted with decreasing how much protein the hair lost, which basically means it prevented the hair from getting weaker,” he notes. Additionally, Dr. Young recommends high monosaturated fat oils such as argan oil, almond oil, and olive oil for hair loss, since these can provide the scalp and hair with deep nourishment and protect against oxidative stress.
