Strictly pro reveals he's engaged and expecting first child with Love Island star

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 23, 2024

STRICTLY pro Neil Jones and Love Island star Chyna Mills are engaged and expecting their first child together.

The couple have revealed Chyna is 13 weeks pregnant and their baby is due in the autumn.

"It still doesn't feel real," Chyna, 24, told Hello! as she shared the news of her impending marriage and motherhood.

Neil, 40, popped the question while on holiday with Chyna in Bali recently.

"I'd already planned to ask Chyna to marry me before we knew she was pregnant. Then we found out and I was like: 'Oh, this is brilliant, everything is coming together,'" Neil told the publication.

Chyna added that it took her a few moments to accept his proposal because she wasn't sure if it was genuine.

 "At first, I thought he was joking because we play jokes on each other all the time," Chyna laughed.
