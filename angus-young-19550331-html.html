Angus McKinnon Young is an Australian musician, best known as the co-founder, lead guitarist and musician of Australian hard rock band AC / DC. He is known for his energetic performances, his school uniform and the duck version of Chuck Berry. Young is ranked 24th on Rolling Stone Magazine's list of the 100 Greatest Guitarists of All Time. In 2003, Young and the rest of AC / DC were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
