Photos of Supermodel Through The Years Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

British model, Cara Delevingne, made her first runway appearance in 2011 London Fashion Week. She also walked down the catwalk as a model at the 2012 Victoria’s Secret fashion show.

Cara was the face of various Burberry campaigns and she’s been featured in advertising campaigns for other brands including H&M Authentic Collection, Dominic Jones Jewelry, Blumarine, Zara and Chanel. Cara was featured on the covers of Vogue UK, Vogue Korea, and i-D, Russh, just to name a few. Cara has made countless appearances on the runway since then and the model has also dipped her foot in acting. Aside from her resume, Cara has her own amazing fashion sense and the model is always rocking some sort of sexy and grungy look.
