Evan Dunfee- Net Worth, Age, Ethnicity, Wife, Height, Weight, Career

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 5, 2024

Evan Dunfee is a Canadian Olympian and race walker. He first set Canadian records in the 50-kilometer race walk (at 3:41:38) at the 2016 Summer Olympics, when he finished fourth. He later made history by winning bronze in the 50km race walk at the 2019 World Athletics Championships, becoming Canada’s first-ever medalist at the global biennial event. At the 2020 Summer Olympics, he surged to the third position and won the bronze medal, becoming only the third Canadian racewalker to win an Olympic medal in the 50 km event.
