Frankie &amp; Bennys brings back its iconic 2003 menu - for a limited time

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

THE Noughties is an era we're all nostalgic about, whether it's the fashion, music or even the food.

Italian-American chain Frankie & Benny's is satisfying customer's nostalgia, with the return of the 'Bring it Back' menu for anyone hoping to indulge in the early 2000s-inspired feast.

The throwback menu launched back in April but is still available for diners who are keen to get a blast from the past - but only for a limited time.

We’ve seen the menu and diners interested in loading up on some much-loved food favourites from yesteryear are in for a treat.

The menu pays homage to the iconic dishes from Frankie & Benny's from the year 2003 - brought back by popular demand.

The restaurant chain is aiming to capture the warm spirit of the early 2000s, with a selection of mouth-watering dishes that have made a triumphant return in 2023.

After all, 2023 is the year known for its wave of nostalgia for the same era, from the S Club 7 reunion tour to the resurgence of LimeWire and Y2K fashion.

The menu will be available to diners until July 4, giving you enough time to get booked in for the ultimate throwback dining experience.

Identifying the cost of living crisis and the need for affordability whilst dining out, a range of special offer prices will be available on drinks and dishes on the menu.

Wondering what has made a comeback? We've got the scoop on all of the popular dishes that customers are bound to love.

Customers looking for the ultimate crowd-pleaser should look no further than the iconic Mamma's Cheesebake.

Among the ingredients are a delicious blend of fine cheeses, accompanied by Mamma's Napoletana sauce. It's served with warm ciabatta bread which is perfect for dipping and a great option to kick off the feast.

If you're after a bigger shared experience that the table can enjoy, Benny's Sampler Platter is an ideal choice and one reminiscent of early noughties birthdays, or Friday night hangouts.

The platter is loaded with a mixture of BBQ chicken wings, chicken strips, garlic ciabatta bread, loaded cheese potato skins and onion rings so there's truly something for everyone.

If that wasn't enough, this sizeable sharing platter is served with sour cream, BBQ and a selection of hot sauce dips.

For thirsty adults, the Purple Rain cocktail is the perfect summer tipple. Boasting lashings of Smirnoff vodka, mixed with blue curacao syrup and sours, topped with lemonade.

To really indulge in the days gone by, it doesn't get more noughties than a Coke Float, does it? Enjoy a cold glass of Coca-Cola topped with a scoop of smooth vanilla ice cream - a refreshing summer treat.

But that's not all, the menu is packed with tasty favourites at affordable prices, you can view the full Bring it Back menu here.

We chatted to Andreia Harwood, Marketing Director at Frankie & Benny's. "Nostalgia is currently igniting conversations all over the UK," she told us.

"Whether we're fondly reminiscing about fashion faux pas or reciting lines from our favourite shows, we are immersed in all things Nineties and Y2K."

"We wanted to tap into those core memories this year, at a time when we’re all craving a generous, delicious dose of nostalgia which inspired the Bring It Back nostalgia menu."
