Xian Lim Biography

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 31, 2024

Xian Lim is a multi-talented personality but is mostly famous as a Filipino-American actor. Besides acting he is also a model, singer, host, blogger, filmmaker, and professional basketball player. As of now, he is working as an actor for GMA Network. 

Lim's ethnicity is Chinese-Filipino and his nationality is Filipino. He was born on July 12, 1989. As of now, he is in his mid-30s. He is originally from San Francisco, California, United States. His parents are Godofredo Lim-Uy and Mary Anne Cruz Lim.

Educational Qualification of  Godofredo Lim-Uy Son Xian 

Godofredo Lim-Uy son Xian graduated high school in Daly City, California, and returned to the Philippines to continue his further studies. He attended college as a basketball varsity scholar at the University of East in Manila.

Before graduating from college Godofredo Lim-Uy's son Xian became involved in modeling and acting.
