Mike Finnegan Considers Himself Lucky Because His Beloved Wife Chose Him

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, October 6, 2024

Mike Finnegan is a well-known American writer, producer, and host of shows like Roadkill and Finnegan's Garage. He has garnered esteem as a columnist for Roadkill Magazine.

Although he does not reveal much about his family life on social media, he often uploads posts featuring his wife Jessa Finnegan.

He considers himself fortunate that his spouse chose him among other men.

Mike Finnegan and Wife's Relationship

Mike basks in the love of his wife Jessa, who he married on September 17, 2005, in an intimate ceremony.

Not much else is known about his wedding besides the fact that they got married on the Colorado River banks. The secrecy is a deliberate effort to protect his family's privacy because of his huge fan following.

Finnegan and his spouse Jessa Finnegan on their wedding day. (Photo: Instagram) 

"The fact is it’s super easy to figure out where we live and although it’s extremely flattering when folks stroll up to my garage door, it also feels a bit sketchy sometimes," Mike explained in an Instagram post dated September 2017.

He did, however, raved about his other half, reasoning that their 15th wedding anniversary was a special day to share something about their marriage. 

He called himself lucky to be her spouse, recalling she was sought after by many. "My wife (her name is Jessa) has never done anything but support the ridiculous adventure our life has become," he added. 

Besides supporting her husband, Jessa works as an account manager at Shaw Contract since April 2015.

Mike Finnegan's Special Acts of Love for Wife

Despite being secretive about his family life, Mike has featured Jessa on his YouTube channel a few times. He uploaded a video that showed him gifting a car to his spouse on their wedding anniversary.

Mike had not been home for much of his 15th wedding anniversary because of work obligations and drag racing activities.

But that did not stop him from celebrating it in a special way.

Mike cut his trip short and went home when his car broke down at Rocky Mountain Raceweek 2.0 on the first day of the competition to celebrate with Jessa. 

On their 11th wedding anniversary, Mike had brought Jessa to tears when he surprised her by renewing his wedding vows in a magnificent ceremony on St. Simons Island.

Mike Finnegan and Jenna Finnegan Have To Kids

Mike and Jenna are parents of two kids.

Their first son, Michael Finnegan, was born in July 2011, and their second son, Rowan Garrett Finnegan, joined the family on September 19, 2014.

Family Time: Finnegan with his wife Jenna and their kids. (Source: Instagram)

His elder son loves surfing, so the Roadkill host often indulges him by accompanying him to the waves.

Mike and Jenna live together with their kids in their own house in Georgia.

The television personality balances the time for his family with his work.

With the world championships under his belt, Mike is a self-taught motor maker, hot rod builder, snowboarder, wake surfer, and drag boat racer.

He is popularly known for Roadkill, SEMA Week 2014, and SEMA Week 2015.
