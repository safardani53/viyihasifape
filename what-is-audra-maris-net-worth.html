What is Audra Mari's net worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

AUDRA Mari made headlines in 2016 after she won the Miss World America title.

Now, in January 2022, she is back in the headlines after her engagement with Josh Duhamel was announced.

What is Audra Mari's net worth?

Born January 8, 1994, Mari, 28, is known as an American model, television host, and beauty pageant titleholder.

She has also represented North Dakota at the Miss Teen USA and Miss USA pageants, where she placed 2nd.

At this time, the exact net worth of Mari is unknown, however, it is reportedly between $1million and $5million.

Who is Audra's fiancé Josh Duhamel?

Duhamel, who is also from North Dakota, announced his engagement to Mari on January 8, 2022, which was also her 28th birthday.

"It’s on!! She found a message in a bottle that washed ashore and said YES!!" he captioned an Instagram post.

Born November 14, 1972, Duhamel, 49, is famously known as an American actor and former fashion model who was also married to singer, Fergie from 2009 to 2019.

Over the years, he has starred in a number of productions including the films Transformers, Love, Simon, Safe Haven as well as the show, All My Children.

Prior to his role in the entertainment industry, Duhamel competed in multiple modeling competitions and famously won the 1997 International Modeling and Talent Association (IMTA) competition, whereas Ashton Kutcher reportedly placed second.

He also appeared as an extra in Donna Summer's music video for I Will Go With You, as well as Christina Aguilera's video for Genie in a Bottle.

As of January 2022, Duhamel has an estimated net worth of $18million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Does Audra Mari have any children?

Along with becoming the next Mrs. Duhamel, Mari will also become a stepmom.

While she does not have any children of her own, Duhamel is the father of 8-year-old Axl Jack, whom he welcomed in 2013 with Fergie.

At this time, it remains unclear if the newly-engaged couple plan on having any children together in the future.

Prior to her relationship with Duhamel, Mari was in a two-year relationship with Property Brothers' Jonathan Scott.
