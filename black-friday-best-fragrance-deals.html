When is Black Friday 2021?

Black Friday is taking place on November 26, so make sure you make a note in your diary now.

It's one of the biggest shopping events of the year, falling on the day after Thanksgiving in the US, making it the perfect time to pick a gift ahead of Christmas.

Leading up to Black Friday and Cyber Monday, we'll be sorting through some of the different retailers' perfume deals to bring a selection of the best prices out there.

When is Cyber Monday?

Cyber Monday takes place on November 29 this year, with most deals lasting over the weekend.

In recent years retailers have released deals in the week of Black Friday, so there's plenty of time to pick up a huge saving this November.

Is Black Friday a good time to buy a fragrance or perfume?

It's an excellent time to treat yourself to a new scent as there are so many Black Friday fragrance and perfume deals around.

If you're really well prepared, you can also use the opportunity to get ahead on your Christmas shopping – perfume gift sets are always in the sales too.

In the run-up to the sales, see if you can do a bit of detective work to find out what fragrances your loved ones are after.

By the time Christmas rolls around, they'll have completely forgotten you ever had the conversation and will think you're a mind-reader!

What are the best perfumes and fragrances you can buy in 2021?

Looking for a new perfume? Why not try Givenchy Irresistible, Miss Dior Rose N'Roses, Gucci Guilty Love Edition, Nars Audacious, Yves Saint Laurent Atlas Garden and Chloé Jasminum Sambac.

One of our favourite releases of last year was Coach Dreams Eau de Parfum – it's supposed to remind you of a Californian road trip with notes of bitter orange, gardenia, musk, cactus flower and Joshua Tree.

This year we've been loving Hugo Boss Alive, Giorgio Armani My Way, Jo Malone Scarlet Poppy Intense, and Valentino's Voce Viva.

If you can find any of these newly released fragrances at a discount, be ready to snap them up.

What were the best Black Friday fragrance deals last year?

Last year a number of fragrance retailers took part in Black Friday, announcing deals ahead of the big day.

All Beauty had up to 65% off fragrances, whilst Boots had better than half price offers

Shoppers could also find deals at The Fragrance Shop, The Perfume Shop had up to 15 per cent off and Superdrug had 50% off selected fragrances from best-selling brands like Versace and Gucci.
