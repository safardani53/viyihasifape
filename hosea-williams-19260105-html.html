Hosea Lorenzo Williams is an American civil rights leader, activist, minister, entrepreneur, philanthropist, scientist and politician. He is perhaps best known as a trusted member of renowned civil rights activist and Nobel Peace Prize winner Martin Luther King Jr. Under the banner of their flagship organization, the Southern Catholic Leadership Conference, King relied on Williams to organize and incite the masses to take nonviolent direct action in countless anti-nonviolent campaigns. public, racial, political, economic, and social. King alternately calls Williams, his lieutenant in the field, his "cow in a porcelain cabinet" and his "Castro". Vowing to continue King's work for the poor, Williams is known as the founding chairman of one of North America's largest social services organizations, Hosea Feed the Hungry and Homeless. Its famous motto is "Don't buy and don't throw".
