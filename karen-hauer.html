Career

She auditioned for season 6 of the US reality program ‘So You Think You Can Dance’ in 2009. Hauer reached the Top 20 before being eliminated in the fifth week. She debuted as a Professional Dancer on ‘Strictly Come Dancing’ in 2012. She appeared in several seasons of ‘Strictly Come Dancing’ with various dance partners. Later, in the Seventeenth Series of ‘Strictly Come Dancing,’ she was matched with comedian Chris Ramsey. They were in fourth place at the time. She also won the World Mambo Championship.

Who is Karen Hauer dating? Karen Hauer is now engaged to opera singer David Webb. She had already been married twice. Her first marriage ended in divorce with Matthew Hauer in 2009. Her second marriage occurred in 2015 to fellow professional dancer Kevin Clifton, and they divorced in 2018.
