Stars Scream for Ice Cream! See Kim Kardashian, Bella Hadid, More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 24, 2024

I scream, you scream, these stars scream for ice cream! Nothing is better than indulging in some ice cream, especially as temperatures rise, and these celebrities couldn’t agree more.

For example, during a trip to Brooklyn eatery L&B Spumoni Gardens in July 2020, model Bella Hadid helped herself to some ice cream with a pal and posted a photo of the pair’s frozen treats on her Instagram Stories.

Three years earlier, during another jaunt to New York in July 2017, the Washington, D.C., native strolled down the street with a cup of Kith ice cream in tow. Though the establishment is perhaps best known for selling pricey sneakers and athletic wear, ice cream, milkshakes and sundaes are also available for purchase.

Across the globe, Camilla, Duchess of Cornwall, and pal Judi Dench feasted on matching ice cream cones in July 2018 during the royal’s visit to the U.K.’s Isle of Wight.

As it turns out, the duchess is quite an ice cream fiend. She also enjoyed sampling the sweet indulgence during an engagement in the U.K. with husband Prince Charles in July 2012. The royal couple ordered matching vanilla cones, but ate them with spoons so as to avoid making a mess. How regal!

Kim Kardashian is yet another repeat ice cream offender. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star nibbled on an ice cream cone at Scott Disick‘s low-key California birthday celebration amid the coronavirus pandemic in May 2020, and also enjoyed some of the refreshing treat while walking with then-husband Kanye West in the Big Apple in April 2012.

However, despite her obvious proclivity for ice cream, Kardashian doesn’t like just any version of the popular food. “Haagen-Dazs is my biggest indulgence,” the Selfish author told Harper’s Bazaar in 2015. “Their dulce de leche flavor is my favorite thing in life.”
